OBSOLETE - As of June 2023, Cenvar Roofing no longer uses Enhancify

Press the link to open the Enhancify website.

The customer will follow the steps and fill out the application directly on the Enhancify website.

The customer will be asked to choose which of the 3 types of financing options they would like to apply for:

  • Direct Funding
  • Indirect Funding
  • Credit Cards

The 3 financing options are:

Direct Funding  (Through Power Pay)

Pros

  • Up to 15 year payment terms
  • Credit Scores of 600 and above
  • Rates of 4.99-12.99%
  • Once job is complete and the homeowner verifies the completion the loan agency funds us Directly

Cons

  • Loan application is more detailed with more verification
  • More work for the sales person

Indirect Funding (Wide Spectrum of Personal Loan Lending institutions)

Pros

  • Approves credit scores as low as 500
  • Terms up to 7 years
  • Rates that can be as low as 4.9% (very rare)
  • Loan application is simple with very little verification
  • Loan approval is quick
  • Funded Directly to homeowner within days of approval
  • Not much involvement from the sales person

Cons

  • Rates can Exceed 30%
  • Origination fees are often 3-5% and sometimes more

Credit Card option 

Pros

  • 0% interest rate up to 18 months
  • Application process is simple

Cons

  • Credit Scores above 680
  • If they do not pay in full after the 0% period often the interest exceeds 24%
  • Convenience fee of 3% charged by us when they pay

It is important to continue to emphasize:

  • Financing through Enhancify is most likely NOT the best option for the customer. We want to continue to express this to the customer.
  • BB&T and / or their bank would be the best option for the customer the majority of the time.
