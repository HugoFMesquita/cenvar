The Cenvar Culture Committee (CCC) is a team comprised of a representative from each branch who meet monthly to plan events that focus on 3 main areas:

  1. Employee Engagement - Cenvar Together
    • Birthdays
    • Holidays
    • Team events
  2. Community Involvement - Cenvar Gives
    • Habitat for Humanity
    • Volunteering in the community
    • Service opportunities
  3. Health / Wellness / Active Lifestyle - Cenvar Fit
    • Fitness challenges
    • Sports events
    • Healthy discussions
