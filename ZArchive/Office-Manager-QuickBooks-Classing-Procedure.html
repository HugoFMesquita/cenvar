Office Manager QuickBooks Input for each job:

When a customer job is invoiced, the Office Manager (OM) is responsible to input the following information in QuickBooks from Job Progress (JP):

  1. Sales Rep
  2. Job Type
  3. Color
  4. Project Coordinator Name
  5. Customer Phone Number
  6. Customer Email Address

Once the job has been invoiced in Job Progress, the job should automatically 'come over' to QuickBooks, and will be ready for data entry.

Office Manager Data Entry Process:

  1. Open QuickBooks.
  2. Click 'customers' located at the top of the toolbar.
  3. Select 'Customer Center' from the dropdown menu.
  • Type the customer name into the 'search' box. This will display the customer just invoiced.
  • Click the 'pencil' to edit the customer's information. (see image)
  • Using the information from Job Progress, fill in the phone # and email.
  • Press 'Ok' when finished entering all of the required data.
  • Double click the correct invoice line that that is displayed under the customer. (see image)
  • Fill in the following data (see image):
    • Sales Representative
    • Job Type
    • Color
    • Project Coordinator
  • Press the blue 'Save & Closed' button when finished. (see image)
