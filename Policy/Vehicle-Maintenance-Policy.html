Additional Truck, Vehicle, Automobile, & Asset Information

Maintenance Schedule Reminders

Regularly scheduled Emaint maintenance work orders will automatically be populated and sent via email according to the following schedule:

  • For most trucks, an Emaint oil change work order is populated every six weeks
  • Trailers require 2-month maintenance every 8-12 weeks
  • Equiptors follow manufacturer's instructions for maintenance
  • (All vehicles) Every twelve months, an Emaint yearly vehicle inspection work order is populated.

Completion of Work Orders

  • Each work order must be completed within 2 weeks.
    • Sign the completed work order and send via email to aimee@cenvarroofing.com by close of business Friday, within 2 weeks of the work order date.

Every work order that is not completed will be fined $50 per week, per work order.

  • If an Emaint work order is unable to be completed within 2 weeks due to a delay at the repair shop, the General Manager is required to notify Corporate via Google Chat (Aimee Camm) by close of business Friday, within 2 weeks of the work order date to request a 1 week date extension. Any work order that does not have an extension is considered late and will incur the $50 fine per week.

All Emaint work order late fees will be charged quarterly, and will be deducted from the General Manager's quarterly bonus check.

It is encouraged to set up payment terms with your mechanic for branch asset service invoices (truck, van, trailer, Equipter, forklift, etc.). However, if your mechanic does not have the capability to set up payment terms and requires immediate Credit Card payment, the itemized paid in full maintenance invoice is required to be posted in the Asset Maintenance Receipts Google Chat channel to alert both the Corporate Finance department, and Operations team. All maintenance invoices will be attached to the asset in Emaint.

Routine Maintenance

Company vehicles:

  • should be kept neat and organized
  • should be washed as needed
  • have a clear pouch in the glove box with an insurance card, vehicle registration, and an accident report form
  • should have no trash, papers, or other materials on the dashboard

All trash (cups, receipts, food bags, etc.) should be cleaned out of each company vehicle at the end of each work day.

Manufacturer Safety Recall Policy

Manufacturer Safety Recall notices will be sent as an Emaint Work Order to the manager who is currently assigned to the asset.

Safety Recall work orders are required to be processed within the same 2 week timeframe as regularly scheduled maintenance work orders. (see above)

Managers are required to schedule all safety recall appointments with the appropriate manufacturer dealership.

The completed safety recall Emaint work order is required to be emailed to Corporate with all recall completion documentation, within 2 weeks of the Emaint date. 

  • Sign the completed work order and send via email to aimee@cenvarroofing.com by close of business Friday, within 2 weeks of the work order date.

The safely recall completion documentation is imperative for liability purposes, and will be uploaded to the asset in Emaint.

If the specific recall parts are not in stock with the dealership, Managers are required to update Corporate weekly with the recall status to be documented on the asset in Emaint.

  • Managers are required to notify Corporate via Google Chat (Aimee Camm) by close of business Friday, within 2 weeks of the work order date to request a 1 week date extension. Any work order that does not have an extension is considered late and will incur the $50 fine per week.
  • Corporate will push out the date of the Emaint work order 1 week to ensure no late fines are incurred.
