Cenvar Roofing Expense Policy

  • When using a personal car for approved business-related travel, Cenvar Roofing will reimburse the employee using the current IRS standard mileage rate. https://www.irs.gov/tax-professionals/standard-mileage-rates
  • Approved personal expenses may be reimbursed in the form of a check. Each purchase must have prior manager approval and be submitted using a Cenvar Roofing expense report. The expense report and all purchase receipts should be emailed to Mary Martin in the accounting department: mary@cenvarroofing.com.
  • Each General Manager shall be authorized to spend up to $100 per month in food and drinks for crew or customers and is authorized to make tool purchases up to $2,000.
  • All reimbursable expenses must have purchase receipt backup and will be subject to inquiry.
  • All employees are required to turn in credit card receipts for every credit card transaction. Receipts are required to be uploaded to the Credit Card Receipts Google Drive folder by the last day of every month.
  • Weekly expense reports must be submitted to managers with purchase receipts for reimbursement by Monday at 5 pm. Corporate will process report reimbursement checks weekly. Expense reports can be formatted using the Weekly Expense Report form (saved as a PDF with receipts attached) or by using the app Expensify.
  • Effective 02/01/2025: All receipt should be uploaded to the Expensify app and submitted no later than the end of the week the expense was incurred.

Helpful hints:

  • Electronic receipts can be saved as PDFs
  • Photo copies of hardcopy receipts are acceptable
  • Subscriptions can use downloaded copies of electronic receipts

Instructions for Expensify

  1. Download the app, click "Continue with google," use your work email. Click Submit, Send Expenses to anyone

 

You may need to select Setting and ensure you are using “classic Expensify.” Newer versions of the app do not support the following steps/procedures.

 

  1. Once you're in, click the + sign in the upper right corner.

 

 

 

  1. Click Manually Create.

 

 

 

 

  1. Fill in all the info for the transaction and attach the receipt as shown below and be sure to select upload the picture of the receipt. Press Save.

 

 

 

  1. Once you have done this for all your receipts for the week, you can click on the 3 lines in the upper left- corner and click Reports.

 

         

 

  1. Click the + sign in the upper right corner, then press Expense Report.

           

 

  1. In the upper left- corner, press Add Expense. All of the receipts you have uploaded should be available here for you to select.

 

                 

 

  1. Once you are done, press Submit Report. Then put in your manager's email address and CC payables@Ravnecroofing.com. Make sure to press “Attach PDF.”

 

 


NOTE: Each manager must look at the expense log on Mondays to ensure that all of the receipts are in for purchases on their card. There is a checkbox for them to select the ones that they have approved. Anything left unchecked will come out of that employee's paycheck, which is the amount at the top.
