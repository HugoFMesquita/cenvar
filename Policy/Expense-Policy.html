Cenvar Roofing Expense Policy

When using a personal car for approved business-related travel, Cenvar Roofing will reimburse the employee using the current IRS standard mileage rate. https://www.irs.gov/tax-professionals/standard-mileage-rates

Approved personal expenses may be reimbursed in the form of a check. Each purchase must have prior manager approval and be submitted using a Cenvar Roofing expense report. The expense report and all purchase receipts should be emailed to Mary Martin in the accounting department: mary@cenvarroofing.com.

Each General Manager shall be authorized to spend up to $100 per month in food and drinks for crew or customers and is authorized to make tool purchases up to $2,000.

All reimbursable expenses must have purchase receipt backup and will be subject to inquiry.

Effective June 1, 2023: All employees are required to turn in credit card receipts for every credit card transaction. Receipts are required to be uploaded to the Credit Card Receipts Google Drive folder by the last day of every month.

Effective September 11, 2023: Weekly expense reports must be submitted to managers with purchase receipts for reimbursement by Monday at 5 pm. Corporate will process report reimbursement checks weekly. Expense reports can be formatted using the Weekly Expense Report form (saved as a PDF with receipts attached) or by using the app Expensify.

Helpful hints:

  • Electronic receipts can be saved as PDFs
  • Photo copies of hardcopy receipts are acceptable
  • Subscriptions can use downloaded copies of electronic receipts

Instructions for Expensify

1-Download the app, Click "Continue with google", use your work email. Click “Submit, Send Expenses to anyone

2-Once you're in, you can start uploading by clicking the + sign in the upper right corner


3-Press Manually Create


4-Fill in all the info for the transaction and attach the receipt as shown below. Press Save


5- Once you have done this for all your receipts for the week, you can click on the 3 lines in the upper left-hand corner and click “Reports”

6-Click the + Sign in the Upper Right Corner, then press Expense Report

7-In the upper left-hand corner, press “Add Expense”. All of the receipts you have uploaded 

should be available here for you to select.

8-Once you are done, press “Submit Report”. Then put in your manager's email address and CC Mary so that she can file all the reports for each week. Make sure to press “Attach PDF”


9-each manager will then have to look at the expense log on Mondays to ensure that all of the receipts are in for purchases on their card. There is a checkbox for them to select the ones that they have approved. Anything left unchecked will come out of that employee's paycheck which is the amount at the top.
