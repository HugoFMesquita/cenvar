Cenvar Roofing Expense Policy

When using a personal car for approved business related travel, Cenvar Roofing will reimburse the employee using the current IRS standard mileage rate. https://www.irs.gov/tax-professionals/standard-mileage-rates

Approved personal expenses may be reimbursed in the form of a check. Each purchase must must have prior manager approval, and be submitted using a Cenvar Roofing expense report. The expense report and all purchase receipts should be emailed to Mary Martin in the accounting department: mary@cenvarroofing.com.

Each General Manager shall be authorized to spend up to $100 per month in food and drinks for crew or customers, and is authorized to make tool purchases up to $2,000.

All reimbursable expenses must have purchase receipt backup, and will be subject to inquiry.
