The Ravnec Roofing Supply Team has a goal to deliver jobs as timely and efficiently as possible. All questions related to material distribution or assets must be posted in the Distribution/Asset Google Chat channel.

 

Updates:

  • Ravnec Roofing Supply will procure all material for Roanoke.
  • Ravnec Roofing supply will deliver materials in Charlottesville.
  • All Material Lists (other than a few miscellaneous items needed when a job runs short) must only be sent to sales@ravnecsupply.com by the Sales Representative, Office Manager or the General Manager.

Distribution Materials:

  • Distribution will stock OSB.
  • Distribution will stock H-clips.
  • Distribution will not stock 1x6 or 1x8’s.
  • Branches are responsible to source any “oddball'' items that are on the material list.

Material List Submission: The Material Distribution team will will work hard to accommodate all fluid scheduling requests.

Remember to:

  • The complete material list must be sent immediately when the job is hard scheduled.
  • Email all material lists to sales@ravnecsupply.com.
  • Include the material drop off notes.
  • Include the "deliver by" date.
  • Include the job "install" date.

Material List Procedure:

All Material Lists (other than a few miscellaneous items needed when a job runs short) must only be sent to the Material Manager by the Sales Representative, Office Manager or the General Manager. 

  1. Send all material lists to: sales@ravnecsupply.com
  2. Project Coordinators cannot request full job orders.
  3. Warehouse workers cannot fill orders without orders being entered into inventory software.
  4. All Material Lists must have a "Deliver by" date and also a "Job start date".
  5. Distribution must have at least 3 full business days to deliver each job. (see example below)
    • Deliver 1/2, 1/3, or 1/4. Job Install Date: 1/53
  6. Distribution will deliver all orders falling under these criteria within the delivery zip code list for each branch. Zip Code Zones Spreadsheet
    1. Orders outside of these zip codes will default to having the crew pick-up at the shop. These will be staged by the "deliver by" date to the branch (Will Call).
    2. All branch Will Call orders must be submitted with a 24 hours' notice.
  7. These criteria apply to both staged jobs (full orders and components) and to delivered jobs.
  8. All requests made outside of these criteria MUST be requested to and approved via email by Materials Manager.
  9. Materials Manager will have until E.O.B the day following the request to reply to the request.

 

  • Exceptions:
    • Jobs that are "thrown in" at the last moment can be filled for Will Call pick-up providing the following criteria are met:
      1. Order has been sent by OM or GM to Material Manager and ample time is provided for order entry and picking order.
      2. All branch Will Call orders must be submitted with a 24 hours notice.
      3. The volume of these requests must be kept to a minimum as determined by the Material Manager: sales@ravnecsupply.com.
      4. In the General Manager's absence (vacation), the acting General Manager may send the Material List.
      5. Any and all requests outside of these criteria may be requested via email to the Material Manager.
      6. It is up to the discretion of the Material Manager whether or not to accommodate requests based on current workload and efficiency considerations. See the below examples.

 

Examples:

Example 1:

The Material List for a job is sent by the OM or GM to sales@ravnecsupply.com before E.O.B on Monday, which has a "Deliver by" date for that Friday. Distribution must have the order delivered by E.O.B on Thursday.

Example 2:

The schedule got shifted, and the crew is running ahead. The OM or GM sends the material list for a job to sales@ravnecsupply.com on Monday for a "Deliver by" date of Thursday. The Om or GM must request this exception. The Material Manager has until the E.O.B on Tuesday to approve or deny the request. If denied, the order will be staged by E.O.B Wednesday at the branch for crew Will Call.

Example 3:

The Material List is sent by the OM or GM to the Material Manager for a job outside of the delivery zip code zones. The OM or GM expects the warehouse to the fill the order and place the materials at the branch for Will Call crew pickup.
