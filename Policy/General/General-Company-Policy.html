Cenvar Roofing Employee Handbook is a document containing important employee information. This document must be signed by all employees.

As detailed in the Uniform Policy, a professional image will be maintained, including the following:

  • Uniforms will be worn from the waist up. No badly torn pants or shorts are allowed. Neatly trimmed facial hair is allowed. Employees must not appear disheveled and must shave daily. We will not take off our shirts.
  • All hats that are worn by any Cenvar employee while on the job must be either Cenvar issued or 100% unbranded, logo-ed, or messaged in any way.
  • All staff must maintain a neat appearance.
  • All staff must maintain neatly trimmed hair and facial hair.
  • Crew members are allowed to cut the sleeves off their T-shirts, but they must stay outside of the shoulder seams and not cut huge gaping holes.
  • All subcontractors are required to wear Cenvar Roofing apparel.
  • Upon quitting or being fired, employees must turn in all uniforms and company property before their last check is released.

No Smoking while working. Smoke breaks must be taken by the trash trailer, and cigarette butts go into the trash trailer.

No Smoking in trucks.

No Cursing.

No Catcalling.

We do not play radios to keep from being annoying to the customers and/or their neighbors.

We have zero tolerance for illegal substance use by our employees. Drug use of any kind anywhere will result in immediate dismissal.

No roofing side work for anyone except close relatives and friends.

Don't give referrals for other businesses while on the clock.

All staff must arrive to work on time, at the time set by management.

Employees are required to keep company cell phones on their person while working so office staff can contact them.

Each employee is required to know and follow the Safety Policy.

All Cenvar Roofing employees must be polite at all times to customers and other employees.

Endeavor at all times to give our customers the best experience possible.

Be accountable for making decisions and doing work that it is line with our company mission and values.

Employees are encouraged to contribute to ideas and forming policies that help us be a better company.

All employees (except Sales Representatives) are required to attend the branch weekly team meetings. See Team Meetings / Branch Meetings / Company Meeting Policy.

Late policy is up to the discretion of the branch GM.

Corporate will be auditing Job Progress files to make sure that processes are being followed, date is being uploaded and entered correctly, and will use the Discipline Written Warning form when processes are not being followed.

Upon the signing of a contract for a full roof replacement, we will put a temporary patch over an existing active leak until the scheduled full roof replacement.

All notes recorded in Job Progress should be recorded under the “Job Notes”  section regardless of what type of note it is. This is so all notes are in one central location, to create a timeline / timestamp with username and data / time of note. 

Failure to follow and adhere to these policies my result in termination of employment.
