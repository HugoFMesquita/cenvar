The term escalation refers to the action whereby the contact information and situation details are sent on to another employee for resolution.

Customer Escalation Policy

When a customer requests to escalate an issue (complaint, problem, grievance, etc.) above the head of the General Manager and speak with the company owner, the General Manager will:

  1. Provide the Regional Manager's email address to the customer to express their concerns via email.
  2. Relay to the customer that the Regional Manager will be notified of the situation.
  3. Will not promise any action such as the Regional Manager responding via email or visiting the customer's property.

Company Escalation Policy: Employees

If there is a company accountability issue with any other employee, including ones higher up the company chain:

  1. First go to the employee directly and try to resolve the issue.
  2. If the issue is not resolved satisfactorily, bring the issue to the attention of your manager for resolution.

Company Escalation Policy: General Managers

If there is a company accountability issue with any other employee, including ones higher up the company chain:

  1. First go to the employee directly and try to resolve the issue.
  2. If the issue is not resolved satisfactorily, send the issue in your individual Branch-GM  Google Chat channel (not the General Manager channel) so that the department managers can be notified of the issue.
