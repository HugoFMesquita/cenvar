Rain / Snow Day Policy

1st rain/snow day in a week: Project Coordinator (hourly & salaried) have the day off, and are required to work Saturday as a make up.

2nd rain/snow day in a week: Project Coordinator (salaried) must report to the branch and for task assignments per the General Manager:

  • cleaning
  • asset work orders
  • errands
  • go-backs
  • inventory management
  • miscellaneous duties

2nd rain/snow day in a week: Project Coordinator (hourly) are not required to report to the branch on the 2nd rain day, but must check in with the General Manager before their scheduled clock-in time.

The General Manager will make the official call by 6:00 a.m. to determine if the branch will be open or closed due to inclement weather. All positions except salaried and office staff will hold to the rain/snow policy as already described. Salaried and office staff will have the day off (with pay, or at least without consequence) as long as the General Manager has closed the branch.

Each Project Coordinator and Crew truck must keep two brand new 20' x 30' tarps on the truck for use in case of unexpected rain.
