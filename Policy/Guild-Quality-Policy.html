GuildQuality is our third-party customer satisfaction surveying company. They capture feedback from out customers, good, bad, or ugly, and share it with us.

Each branch is required to have an average GuildQuality score of 97% and above.

If a General Manager reviews a branch crossover GuildQuality, they will forward it to the appropriate branch GM for their review.

Note: GuildQuality changed their metrics from a 1 through 4 scale to a 1 through 5 scale. We adjusted our metrics to match.

If you have any GuildQuality questions or concerns, please contact Corporate.
