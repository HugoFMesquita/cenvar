• When a customer calls the call center, the agent shall ascertain if the prospective job is majority flat work or majority steep slope. 
    • Majority steep slope: The lead goes to the residential division. 
    • Majority low slope or flat: It goes to the commercial division.
  • Either division may elect to quote and produce roofing work of any type and ratio. 
  • Sales personnel are not to quote work that their division will not produce. Any work that the division will not produce must be referred to the other division to be quoted and produced.
  • When a sales representative from either division refers work to the other division, they shall be paid a commission of 2% of the gross revenue up to a maximum of $3,500.00 in commission paid for a  single job.
  • If it comes to our attention that the commercial and residential divisions are quoting the same job because of a personal lead, whichever division obtained the lead first shall have the right to bid it, the other will refrain.
