A reclaimed job is any job that is converted to a sale/contract that was previously either:

  1. Archived and flagged as ‘Job Lost’, or
  2. In the Future Work (Quoted) bucket with an expired or non-existent follow-up reminder.

Lost Jobs shall be contacted by the Customer Service Representative (CSR) according to Customer Service Follow Up Calls.

Jobs from the Future Work (Quoted) bucket in Job Progress that have an expired or non-existent follow-up reminder can be contacted by any member of the Call Center team.

Process

When a potential reclaimed job is found, the appropriate Call Center team member should:

  1. Add a follow up reminder for themselves (max 30 days).
  2. Contact the customer.
  3. Include a note in JP about the contact.
  • If a message is left, then indicate a voicemail was left, then set a new reminder.
  • If the customer is no longer in need of service, include a note in JP and archive the job.
  • If the customer requests a later contact date, include a note in JP, then set a new reminder (30 days max, unless otherwise specified by the customer).

Incentive

Any successfully reclaimed job shall pay commission:

  • 75% to the Call Center team member
  • 25% to the original Sales Representative

If the original Sales Representative is no longer employed with Cenvar, the Sales Manager shall assign a new Sales Representative. Commission is then adjusted to:

  • 50% to the Call Center team member
  • 50% to the new Sales Representative
