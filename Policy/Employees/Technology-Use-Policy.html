Company provided computers, tablets, and phones ("devices") are intended to be used by employees for work related purposes. Users of these devices are expected to understand and adhere to the contents of this policy.

Use of Computer Accounts

Employees may only use the computer accounts authorized by the Company for their individual use. Use of another person's account, identity, devices, or presentment of false or misleading information or credentials is prohibited. Behavior of all employees on Company devices shall be consistent with the Employee Conduct Policy and the Employee Handbook.

Management of Company Assigned Devices

Employees are responsible to update software programs used for business purposes.
