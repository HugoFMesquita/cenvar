Cenvar (the “Company”) provides devices to their employees that are to be used for company-related purposes. Devices covered by this policy include computers and mobile devices (tablets and phones). The type of device(s) assigned to an employee will be appropriate for the employee's role.

This policy is designed to protect the Company, the Company's data and systems/software, and the Company's employees. The decision to provide devices for contractors/subcontractors shall be at the discretion of management.

Use of Company Accounts and Devices

Employees may only use the computer accounts authorized by the Company for their individual use. Use of another person's account, identity, devices, or presentment of false or misleading information or credentials is prohibited. 

Behavior of all employees on Company devices shall be consistent with the Employee Conduct Policy and the Employee Handbook.

Company devices should be used for any customer interaction. This includes phone calls, SMS messaging ("text"), chats, video calls, and emails. Innocently copying company data (which includes all prospect/customer information) onto a personal device could lead to unintended legal consequences.

All work using Company software must be conducted on a Company device.

Employees are responsible to update software programs used for business purposes. 

Personal use of a company device should be limited to activities pertaining to family (e.g., checking weather forecasts, school closing information, etc.).

Under no circumstance should a Company device be used for entertainment purposes.

Any Company issued device remains the property of the Company and can be monitored by the Company.

The Company reserves the right to seize devices and all information contained therein in connection with Company investigations into wrongdoing.

Right to Block Content

The Company reserves the right to block communication or additions or applications that are deemed not in compliance with Company Policies, Procedures, and Vision.

Use of Personal Devices for Company Use

Only in rare circumstances should a personal device be used for Company work when adequate devices have been provided. Any questions regarding the use of personal devices need to be addressed with management.

Exception: Contractors/Subcontractors who have not been provided devices under this policy.
