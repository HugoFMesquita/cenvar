General Managers

Required to have their cell phone turned on, and be accessible every Saturday if there are active job sites going on.

Required to touch base with each Project Coordinator who is working on a Saturday.

Project Coordinators

If there is a rain day Monday through Friday during the normal work week, Project Coordinators are required to work their full 5th day on Saturday of the same week.

If it is the 6th day of a 6 day work week, the Project Coordinator can be off for the day when their crew returns from their lunch break.
