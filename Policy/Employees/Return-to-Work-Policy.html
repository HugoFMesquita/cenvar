Any time an employee is injured at home or at work, they are required to obtain a doctor's note releasing them to return to work.

The employee cannot return to work without a release from their attending physician.

The employee must notify their manager according to the Incident Report procedure, and provide them with the doctor's note releasing them to return to work.

The manager must report the injury according to the Incident Report procedure, and provide the doctor's note to the Human Resources Department.
