Forklift Certification is required for:

  • All Cenvar Roofing  Project Coordinators
  • Members of the Repair Crew
  • Shop Crew

Process for Forklift Certification

  • The Cenvar Roofing Human Resources (HR) Department will create a forklift training username/password for each new employee during orientation.
    • Note: HR will provide each new employee a password sheet with all Cenvar Roofing logins during the onboarding process.
  • HR will create a unique username/password for the new employee to sign into  www.certifyme.net to take the Forklift Certification.
  • HR will keep a log of certification dates and will notify the branch General Manager of all employees that have expiring certificates.
  • The branch General Manager will make arrangements for the employee to retake the test in their branch office.
  • The unique username/password can be used to recertify when it expires. Forklift certifications expire every 3 years. The employee can log in from any computer as long as they have their unique username/password that was created during onboarding.
