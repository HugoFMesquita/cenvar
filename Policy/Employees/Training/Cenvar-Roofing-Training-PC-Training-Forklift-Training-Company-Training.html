Project Coordinator Training Program

The Cenvar Roofing Project Coordinator Training Program is professional development program for all PC's to gain knowledge and hands on experience.

Each Project Coordinator (PC) will work for 2 weeks with the Corporate Trainer on the "PC Crew". The PC will experience on the job training while executing shingle & metal roof installation.

Each PC is required to purchase the following 3 items before their first day of training:

  • Tool Pouch (PC to purchase)
  • Hammer (PC to purchase)
  • Tape measure (PC to purchase)
  • Harness (Cenvar Roofing to provide)
  • All other materials will be provided by Cenvar Roofing.

General Coordinator must notify the Corporate Trainer in advance of any days off / time off that their PC has, before the training program starts. This will allow the Corporate Trainer to schedule and plan roofing jobs for 2 weeks of production.

At the end of training, Project Coordinators are required to pass a Cenvar Roofing closed book Owens Corning Technical Aptitude Test.

  • The OC Technical Aptitude Test is a 40 question multiple choice test.
  • Each question is worth 2 points, for a total of 80 possible points.
  • Your General Manager will arrange a date / time for your testing in your branch.

Owens Corning (OC) University

Owens Corning University (OCU) is your direct connection to all things education. Users are able to access mini-modules for learning on the go, and a robust reference library.

Cenvar Roofing Project Coordinators are required to complete all OC University training modules within the first 4 weeks of their hire date AND again within month 6-8 after hire date.
