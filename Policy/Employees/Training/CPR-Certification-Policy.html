General Requirements

General Managers (GM) are responsible to arrange CPR certification and renewals for each required person.

The GM will make arrangements for their employees to take the CPR certification course within their first 90 days of employment.

Any employee who is not CPR certified within their first 90 days of employment will not be allowed to work until certification is completed.

Once an employee has successfully completed their CPR certification, the branch GM is required to send the completed certificates to Human Resources via the Human Resources Google Chat channel.

Human Resources will keep a log of certification dates, and will notify the branch GM of employees that have expiring certificates.

The branch GM will make arrangements for the employee to renew their certification.

Any employee who has an expired CPR certification will not be allowed to work.

General Managers should coordinate CPR/First Aid/AED certification (and re-certification) through the Safety Director, Nate Hertzog, natehertzog@cenvarroofing.com. Cenvar Roofing is now an approved Health & Safety Institute (HSI) Training Center.
