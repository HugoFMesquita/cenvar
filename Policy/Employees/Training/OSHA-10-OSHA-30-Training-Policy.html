OSHA-10 Training Policy

Every Cenvar Roofing Project Coordinator, Repair Crew Leader, and Gutter Crew Leader is required to complete OSHA 10-Hour construction safety training.

General Managers are responsible to arrange OSHA 10-Hour training for each Project Coordinator, Repair Crew Leader, and Gutter Crew Leader.

The GM will make arrangements for their employees to take the OSHA 10-Hour course by coordinating with the Safety Director. The Safety Director will register the employee with OSHAcademy.

Once an employee has successfully completed their OSHA 10-Hour training, the branch General Manager is required to send a copy of the completed certificate to Human Resources via the Human Resources Google Chat channel.

The Safety Director will keep a log of certification dates.

OSHA-30 Training Policy

General Managers, Management, and Executives are required to get OSHA 30-Hour construction safety trained.

Employees will make arrangements to take the OSHA 30-Hour course by contacting the Safety Director.

Once a General Manager, Management, or Executive has successfully completed their OSHA 30-Hour training, they are required to send a copy of the completed certificate to Human Resources via the Human Resources Google Chat channel.

The Safety Director will keep a log of certification dates.

OSHA Retraining

OHSA 10-Hour and 30-Hour do not expire, however, if the training was completed several years prior, the Safety Director may require the employee to re-take the training.

The Safety Director will register the employee with OSHAcademy (oshatrain.org). We have corporate account with them. This account provides limited tracking of completion and discounts.
