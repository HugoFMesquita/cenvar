Missed Telephone Calls

All missed telephone calls, company wide, are required to be returned in a timely manner.

Office Telephone Policies & Procedure

Check the voicemail every morning upon arrival and throughout the day.

If the voicemail is from an existing customer (they have a CRM file):

  • Note the CRM file
  • Send a message to the GM or sales representative via G chat.

If you are unsure of the nature of the call, call back the customer for more information.

    * NOTE: If you are unable to reach the customer, send the information to the GM via G chat. *

Answering calls:

Answer the phone within three rings.

Answer the phone “Cenvar Roofing this is ___________.”

If you receive a call that would be serviced by another branch, write down the customer's name and phone number, then transfer the call to the appropriate branch.

When a call comes in, if they haven’t already stated it, ask for the customer’s name and purpose of their call.

If an existing customer calls (they have a CRM file):

  • If you can answer the question, note the CRM file.
  • If you can’t answer the question:
    • Always transfer the call to the GM or sales representative.
    • Note the CRM file about the call
    • Send a message to the GM or sales representative via G chat.
  • If they want to leave a message:
    • Note the CRM file.
    • Send a message via G chat.

If the caller is NOT a customer (no CRM file):

  • Take down their name, number, and the reason for the call.
  • Tell them you will forward a message to the appropriate team member.
  • Send a message to the appropriate team member via G chat.

   * NOTE: Do NOT promise action on the part of the team member, only that you will pass the message. *

Customer Inquiry Call

When a potential customer calls, ask the customer if they are in need of a new roof estimate or a repair estimate. The customer may also be looking for information pertaining to gutters or a go-back service. Use Scheduling a Sales Appointment and enter the customer information into Job Progress.

Entering a New Customer is to be used whether the customer is looking for a roof replacement, roof repair, gutters, or go-backs.

Upset Customer Calls

Always remain calm, pleasant, and professional.

If a caller is upset, it is company policy to place them on a brief hold and transfer the call immediately to the branch General Manager.

If the General Manager cannot be reached, write down the name and telephone number and ensure the caller that the General Manager will call them as quickly as possible.

Also see the Customer Escalation Policy / Employee Escalation Policy / Complaints.

Follow-up Telephone Calls

Sales Representatives are required to follow up with customers after their sales presentations until the contract / estimate / quote is signed. Refer to the Follow Up Telephone Call Procedure.

Collection Calls

Each branch is responsible to monitor their past due invoices and make collection calls until the invoice is paid in full.

The Collections Policy is to be referred to as a guide for all branch collections.
