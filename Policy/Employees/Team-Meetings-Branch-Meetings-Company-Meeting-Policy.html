Weekly Team Meetings

Each Manager shall have a regularly scheduled weekly meeting (or meetings) that includes every team member who reports to them.

Requirements

  1. Attendance is mandatory.
  2. If a team member will not be able to attend:
    • Manager shall approve prior to the start of the meeting.
    • Manager shall cover the topics from the meeting within one business day.
  3. Managers should determine the time that best suits the team.
  4. Sales meetings can be virtual.

Mandatory Agenda Topics

  1. Safety (Branch meetings only)
  • A safety topic must be included and logged on the Safety Meeting Agenda / Attendance Sheet.

2. Business Topics

  • Cover appropriate general enterprise news and direction from the manager above.
  • Include every applicable item of business that was passed down to manager.
  • Solicit ideas for business improvement.
  • Give feedback from upper management about any ideas that were submitted in the prior weekly meeting.
  • Manager should ask their team what they need in order to succeed in their respective jobs.

3. Changes

  • Solicit questions, concerns, and feedback about recent policy and procedural changes. Any of these that cannot be answered by the manager shall be passed up the command chain for further clarification; the answer will be passed back down in the next week's meeting.
  • Relate the answers to any questions posed by their team from the prior week's meeting.

4. Kudos

  • Go around the room and have each team member say something they appreciate about another team member or relate a way in which a team member helped them.

Quarterly Regional Manager All Call Branch Team Meeting

The Regional Manager will schedule & meet quarterly with each branch for an All Call Branch Team Meeting according to the below EOS Meeting schedule.

The Vision / Traction Organizer (EOS Meetings) used for organizing plans and is a forecast of our current company goals.

1st Quarter EOS Meetings:

  • Corporate Executive EOS meeting- 1st week of December (of prior 4th quarter)
  • General Manager EOS meeting - 2nd week of December (of prior 4th quarter)
  • All Call Branch EOS meeting - 1st week of January (actual 1st Quarter)

2nd Quarter EOS Meetings:

  • Corporate Executive EOS meeting - 3rd week of March (of prior 1st quarter )
  • General Manager EOS meeting - 4th week of March (of prior 1st quarter)
  • All Call Branch EOS meeting - 1st week of April (actual 2nd Quarter)

3rd Quarter EOS Meetings:

  • Corporate Executive EOS meeting - 3rd week of June (of prior 2nd quarter)
  • General Manager EOS meeting - 4th week of June (of prior 2nd quarter)
  • All Call Branch EOS meeting - 1st week of July (actual 3rd Quarter)

4th Quarter EOS Meetings:

  • Corporate Executive EOS meeting - 3rd week of September (of prior 3rd quarter)
  • General Manager EOS meeting - 4th week of September (of prior 3rd quarter)
  • All Call Branch EOS meeting - 1st week of October (actual 4th Quarter)
