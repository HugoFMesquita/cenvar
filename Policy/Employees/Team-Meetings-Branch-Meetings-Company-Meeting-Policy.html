Weekly Team Meetings

The branch General Manager is required to schedule and lead branch weekly team meetings. Safety topics must be included and logged on the Safety Meeting Agenda / Attendance Sheet. All employees (minus Sales Representatives) are required to attend as per the General Company Policy.

Weekly meeting times are not mandated by Corporate.

Weekly meetings are required:

  • To be attended by all teammates (minus Sales Representatives)
  • To include a safety topic with a signed Safety Meeting Agenda / Attendance Sheet to be filed into the safety binder.
  • So that that the General Manager reviews & discusses the items listed in the GM Friday email.
    • Policy and procedural changes will be released as needed. The Executive team will examine the information that is going out each week to determine if there are too many major procedural changes.

In each weekly meeting, every team member will acknowledge a fellow team member to express their appreciation for that person, with details of how they were helpful that week.

Weekly Sales Meetings

The Sales Manager is required to schedule and lead weekly virtual sales meetings.

Sales Representatives are required to attend the weekly virtual sales meeting.

Quarterly General Manager Meetings

The Quarterly General Manager EOS meeting will be held at the Corporate offices according to the below EOS Meeting schedule. 

All General Managers are required to attend. 

The Vision / Traction Organizer (EOS Meetings) used for organizing plans and is a forecast of our current company goals.

1st Quarter EOS Meetings: 

  • Corporate Executive EOS meeting- 1st week of December (of prior 4th quarter)
  • General Manager EOS meeting - 2nd week of December (of prior 4th quarter)
  • All Call Branch EOS meeting - 1st week of January (actual 1st Quarter)

2nd Quarter EOS Meetings: 

  • Corporate Executive EOS meeting - 3rd week of March (of prior 1st quarter )
  • General Manager EOS meeting - 4th week of March (of prior 1st quarter)
  • All Call Branch EOS meeting - 1st week of April (actual 2nd Quarter)

3rd Quarter EOS Meetings: 

  • Corporate Executive EOS meeting - 3rd week of June (of prior 2nd quarter)
  • General Manager EOS meeting - 4th week of June (of prior 2nd quarter)
  • All Call Branch EOS meeting - 1st week of July (actual 3rd Quarter)

4th Quarter EOS Meetings: 

  • Corporate Executive EOS meeting - 3rd week of September (of prior 3rd quarter)
  • General Manager EOS meeting - 4th week of September (of prior 3rd quarter)
  • All Call Branch EOS meeting - 1st week of October (actual 4th Quarter)
