Cenvar Roofing Uniform Policy

Applies to: Sales, GMs, Repairs, Crews, Gutters, Mechanics, and Subcontractors

  • To maintain a professional image, uniforms will be worn from the waist up at all times.
  • No badly torn pants or shorts are allowed.
  • Neatly trimmed facial hair is allowed.
  • Employees must not appear disheveled and must shave daily. We will not take off our shirts.
  • All hats that are worn by any Cenvar employee while on the job must either be Cenvar issued or 100% unbranded, logo-ed, or messaged in any way.
  • Athletic shorts are not allowed for full time staff except for repair team, delivery drivers, and full time warehouse staff.
  • All subcontractors are to wear required Cenvar Roofing Approved apparel.
  • Upon quitting or being fired, employees must turn in all uniforms before their last check is released.

Each new crew member shall be supplied: 5 shirts and 1 hoodie.

Employees shall be supplied with shirts, hoodies, jacket, and coats as needed.

Employees shall be supplied with generic brand apparel. Brand name apparel will be available for extra cost to whoever wants it.
