Employee Evaluations

Managers will evaluate their employees once per year in January using the Employee Evaluation Form.

  • Employees are to complete the Performance Evaluation self-assessment and submit it to their manager no later than the second week of January.
  • Managers are to complete the Performance Evaluation manager assessment and submit it to Human Resources (hr@cenvarroofing.com) no later than the last two weeks of January.

Manager Evaluations

All employees will evaluate their manager on Peakon once per year in the 2nd week of January. The yearly evaluation questions are unique to the manager evaluation. 

  • Note: This has been reduced from twice a year.
