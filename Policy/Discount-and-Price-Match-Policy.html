Discount Policy

We will not offer any discounts other than:

  • Price Match Discount
    • The price match must be for an apples-to-apples comparison.
  • Manager Approved Discount
    • To only be used with a manager's approval.
  • Seasonal Discount
    • We no longer sell winter discounts throughout the year.
  • Damage Discount
    • To be used for damages caused as a result of Cenvar Roofing performed services.

We do not offer senior or military discounts.

Price Match Policy

Sales Representatives should never leave the sales appointment without mentioning our price match guarantee, for both shingles and metal.

The price match must be for an apples-to-apples comparison.

When a price match is used for any job:

  • the Job Progress “price match” flag must be used
  • the quote we are matching to must be uploaded to Job Progress.

Metal price match: if the comparable quote is vague, we will quote a bare bones estimate including synthetic overlay on top of existing shingles, gable trim, ridge cap, and cheap screws (not pro-Z screws).

Discount Procedure

All discounts must be approved by a Branch Manager.

The discount type must be accurately selected on the invoice in Job Progress.

  • Select the correct customer invoice in Job Progress.
  • Click the “pencil” icon to edit the invoice.
  • Click the blue “Add Line” button.
  • Type “Discount” in the new invoice line to display the drop down menu (see image below)
  • Select the correct “trade” and “work type” from the drop down menu.
  • Select the correct discount type from the drop down menu.
  • Press “save”.
  • Enter the correct QTY.
  • Enter the correct “Rate”, discount amount. Example: $100.00 off is entered as -100.00.
  • Type any notes describing the discount details in the “Notes” box.
  • Press the blue “Update” button.
