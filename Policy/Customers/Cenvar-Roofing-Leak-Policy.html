Cenvar Roofing is happy to come out to investigate any leak and we will provide a thorough report on the cause of the leak.

At-fault determination

  • If the leak is deemed to be a failure on Cenvar Roofing's part, we will gladly make all necessary repairs.
  • If the leak is deemed to be caused by anything other than the roof replacement, we will charge a $250.00 service fee to diagnose.

Either way, the customer will receive a report of the cause of the leak, provided by Cenvar Roofing.
