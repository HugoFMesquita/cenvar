Customers must agree to the job color selection by either:

  • Signing the contract with the color selection specified, or
  • Signing the color selection form sent through Job Progress

If the customer signs the contract with no color selection, they must complete the color selection form sent via Job Progress once they select a color.

The customer's job must not be hard scheduled if neither the color selection form or the contract have been signed by the customer.

Office Managers are responsible to verify that the customer has signed and agreed to the color selection via the contract or the color selection form prior to hard scheduling.
