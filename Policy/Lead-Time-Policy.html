Keeping our lead times reasonable is a huge part of powering growth. We track lead times for all sales and production. There are maximum lead time requirements that we will be required to meet 75% of the time and they are measured weekly.

For each category, you can exceed the requirement a total of 13 weeks out of the year and have to stay under the requirements for at least 39 weeks.

Sales (Repairs and Roofs)

There shall be at least 6 openings per sales person in the first 7 business days.

An opening is defined as number of spaces left out of three appointments.

If you go to 4 appointments, you will need to pack them into the earlier days to leave space toward the end.

If there are more than two appointments per salesperson out beyond seven days, it will be a “fail” no matter the number of openings.

Reroofing production

We need to stay within 6 weeks of backlog.

Backlog is defined as the combination of hard and soft schedule.

Repairs production

We need to stay within two weeks backlog.

Gutters

We need to be able to install gutters within 7 days of installing the roof.
