Cenvar Roofing General Knowledge

Employees are required to 

  • Read and sign the Cenvar Roofing Employee Handbook.
  • Know and intimately understand the Cenvar Roofing General Company Policy.
  • Be familiar with the Cenvar Roofing: Mission & Vision.

Lead Development Specialist / Call Center Representatives Policies

  • Are required to know and intimately understand the Job Description: Lead Development Specialist (Call Center).
  • Should refer to the Office Telephone Policy for all telephone calls.
  • Should refer to Scheduling A Sales Appointment for scheduling sales appointments.
  • Should refer to Customer Service Follow Up Calls for customer follow up call information.
  • Should refer to the Reclaimed Jobs Policy for recovering lost jobs.
  • Are required to know and understand the job specific Job Progress Tutorials: Call Center.
  • Are required to return all phone calls / messages that come in prior to 5:00 pm on the same day, working overtime if needed. Calls at 5:00 pm and later can be returned the next business day.

Job Progress Tutorials: All Tutorials is also available for reference.
