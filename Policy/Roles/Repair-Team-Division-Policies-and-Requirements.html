Repair Division Specific Policies & Requirements

The Repair Team:

  • Reports to the branch General Manager
  • Will diagnose, price, and complete roof repairs for customers as needed for each specific job.
  • Should refer to the Job Progress Tutorials: Repairs. Job Progress Tutorials: All Tutorials is also available.
  • Will inform their customers immediately if they get more than 10 minutes behind schedule.
  • Must be prepared with the required tools according to the Cenvar Roofing Metal Tool List and EPDM / Rubber Tool List.
  • Will inform the office if they are running behind and cannot catch up so that their schedule can be rearranged.
  • Will get the repair contract signed on every job before any work is done.
  • Must know the specs in the Owens Corning Roofing University Roofing Fundamentals & Application Training Manual and in the GAF Pro Field Guide.
  • Must install shingle roofs in a manner consistent with the above referenced manuals.
  • Must keep a cell phone on their person while working, so office staff can contact them.
  • Required to take before and after pictures of their work, and upload all photos into Job Progress. Notes are required to be added to each job explaining what the customer issue is and what the repair solution is.
  • Must know and follow all Safety Policies.
  • Must be on time and attend all required team meetings.
  • Should follow the Job Progress Service Proposal - Temporary Tarps or Roof Inspections procedure.

Repair leads are booked according to the Replacement and Repairs Appointments Procedure.

Any full roof replacements done by a Cenvar Roofing repair crew must be reported on the payroll spreadsheet under the ‘Repair Tab’ (bottom).

Repair Sales Representatives will receive payment of 38% across the board on work that they both sell and complete.

A Repair job must be a  minimum of $250.00 for a Repairs crew to be dispatched. The only exception is if a simple repair is done at the time of the sales estimate (replace pipe boot, small caulking job, etc.).

If a repair job is grossly misquoted or underestimated the branch General Manager is allowed to reach out to Allen Branscome for permission to pay additional for the misquoted or misdiagnosed repair job.

The Repair Division will receive 25% pay when doing a replacement.

  • Anything 8 square or more pays 25%
  • Anything 7.9 square or less pays 32%
  • Repair vs. Replacement classing specifics are located in Sales Representative Policies & Requirements.

Partial Roof Replacement proposal specifics located in Sales Representative Policies & Requirements.
