Cenvar Roofing General Knowledge

Office Managers (OMs) are required to:

  • Read and sign the Cenvar Roofing Employee Handbook.
  • Know and intimately understand the Cenvar Roofing General Company Policies.
  • Be familiar with our Cenvar Roofing: Mission & Vision.

Office Manager Specific Knowledge

OMs are required to:

  • Know and intimately understand the Office Manager Employee Contract.
  • Know and understand the Office Manager Job Description.

Office Manager Specific Policies

OMs should refer to the Office Telephone Policy for all telephone calls.

OMs are responsible to keep the branches voicemail current, accurate and in a warm, friendly, human voice.  Office Telephone Policy

OMs should refer to the Scheduling A Sales Appointment for scheduling sales appointments.

OMs are required to keep track of customers that have and have not paid. Receivables Process

OMs are required to inform the customer of our leak policy on all go-back telephone calls. Cenvar Roofing Leak Policy

OMs are required to post the job name & job number of each job that does not go over from Job Progress to Quickbooks on the Office Manager G-Chat Channel.

Job Progress

OMs are required to know and understand the job specific Job Progress Tutorials: Office Manager.

OMs should follow the Job Progress Workflow: Job Progress Workflow

OMs are required to enter a sub-contractor and project coordinator in Job Progress. Job Step by Step Workflow

OMs should follow the Job Progress Service Proposal – Temporary Tarps or Roof Inspections procedure.

OMs must process all “go backs” using the same Job Progress process as a standard job.

OMs are required to check the “work in progress” tab in Job Progress regularly to make sure the status is correct.

OMs are required to add a customer ‘note’ each time that a customer is contacted. Job Step by Step Workflow

OMs will be included in monthly Job Progress Audits. Each month, the Cenvar Roofing accounting department randomly selects Job Progress files to check for user accuracy and user entries.

Materials

OMs are required to request an order confirmation form when orders materials through any vendor.

OMs are required to order materials for upcoming jobs when the job is scheduled per the Material Distribution / Material Delivery material list procedure.

OMs are responsible to monitor and update the Inventory Management Sheet.

Jobs & Job Schedules

OMs are required to move the jobs through the Job Progress workflow. Job Step by Step Workflow

OMs are responsible to keep a list of all jobs on the Job Board, and check off each job as it is Invoiced.

OMs are required to call customers and update them on the status of their job once it is on the schedule.

OMs are responsible for communicating all job schedules & scheduling adjustments to the subcontractor/crew. For branches that do not have an Office Manager, it is the responsibility of the General Manager to communicate the job schedule & scheduling adjustments to the subcontractor/crew. *Please note that the Project Coordinator is still responsible for calling the customer the day prior to the start of the job to confirm the scheduled production date.

OMs are required to communicate daily with each Project Coordinator to obtain job updates.

OMs are required to get updates from PCs by no later than 3pm.

OMs are required to send a photo of the house and the work order, via text or email, to the subcontractor/crew for all jobs. This will help to ensure that the correct house is being worked on. The General Manager can determine who (Office Manager or Project Coordinator) sends the work order and photo of the house to the crews, however whoever sends the work order must also send the photo of the house.

OMs are required to follow the Skylight Policy.

Quickbooks Classing & Unclassed Invoices

OMs are required to class jobs in QuickBooks. See Office Manager QuickBooks Classing Procedure

OMs are required to class each job in Quickbooks, and warranty the job once the job is paid.

  • Office Manager Unclassed Invoices Report
  • Warrantying and Closing a Job In Job Progress

Warranty

OMs are required to register the product warranty once the customer has paid.

The OM will send one warranty email to the customer with all applicable product warranty information attached:

  • roof warranty
  • power fan warranty
  • skylight warranty
