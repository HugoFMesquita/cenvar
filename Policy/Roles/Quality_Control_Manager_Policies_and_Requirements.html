Cenvar Roofing General Knowledge

Quality Control Managers (QCMs) are required to:

  • Read and sign the Cenvar Roofing Employee Handbook
  • Know and intimately understand the Cenvar Roofing General Company Policy
  • Be familiar with our Cenvar Roofing: Mission & Vision

Cenvar Roofing Quality Control Manager Policies and Procedures

For their specific role, QCMs are required to:

  • Know and intimately understand the Subcontractor / Crew Policies
  • Be familiar with the Job Progress Tutorials: All Positions

QCMs will contact the Regional Manager with all subcontractor production issues, not the General Manager (GM).

Repair and gutter department quality inspections / control will be handled at the branch level. Corporate QCMs will not dictate how to inspect. Oversight by the GMs is suggested.
