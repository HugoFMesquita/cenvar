Vice President of Sales / Sales Manager (SM) Policies and Requirements

Policies

  • SMs are required to know and intimately understand the Sales Representative Policies & Requirements
  • SMs are required to schedule, lead, and attend company meetings per the Team Meetings / Branch Meetings / Company Meeting Policy

Requirements for SM

  • Monitor all branch sales materials and will be responsible for ordering all sales material.
  • Responsible for determining when a new sales representative is needed to hire or move up internally.
  • Monitor all Sales Representatives job pricing.
  • Monitor the Sales Representative use of Job Progress.
  • Grant branch-specific access to Sales Representatives in Job Progress when a Sales Representative is assigned to work in a different branch/

Sales Representatives report directly to the Sales Manager.

Requirement for Vice President of Sales

The Vice President of any team is not allowed to approach any team member of another Vice President with directives or requests. If a Vice President is in need of help from the team member of another Vice President, they are required to contact Chris Good. Chris will approach the other Vice President with the request and make the decision.
