All accidents in company vehicles, regardless of severity, must be reported.

First, call 911 to report the accident.

Second, call your General Manager to inform them of the accident.

Per the Cenvar Roofing Vehicle / Truck Use Policy, employees are responsible to report all vehicle / asset damage to their branch General Manager.

General Managers are required to notify Human Resources within 1 hour of any injury incident or vehicle accident on the Human Resources Google Chat channel.

Third, fill out a Cenvar Roofing Vehicle / Asset Accident Report Form (located in the vehicle glove box) and give it to your GM to be sent to the Corporate office immediately. The Human Resource department needs this information to report to our insurance, and the Operations team must make a note that the vehicle was involved in a wreck.

The completed form, with photos of the damage, must be sent to the General Manager within 1 hour of the incident.

If you have been injured as a result of an auto accident, you are required to fill out both the Vehicle / Asset Accident Report form, as well as the First Report of Injury form.
