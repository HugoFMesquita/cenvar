If you are involved in a vehicle / asset accident, you must follow the below procedure.

  • Per the Cenvar Roofing Vehicle / Truck Use Policy, and the Vehicle Accident Policy employees are responsible to report all vehicle / asset damage to their branch General Manager.
  • General Managers are required to notify Human Resources within 1 hour of any injury incident or vehicle accident on the Human Resources Google Chat channel.
  • General Managers must report all vehicle damage, with photos, to Corporate within 24 hours of the occurrence using the Vehicle / Asset Damage Report Form.
  • Corporate will decide if the vehicle / asset will be repaired.
  • General Managers are required to send the completed form, with photos of the damage, to the Human Resources Google Chat channel within 24 hours after the accident.

Vehicle / Asset Damage Report Form

If you have been injured as a result of an auto accident, you are required to fill out both the Vehicle / Asset Accident Report form, as well as the First Report of Injury form.

If you have any questions, please contact your General Manager, or Human Resources: HR@cenvarroofing.com

This form must be kept in every Cenvar Roofing vehicle glove box.
