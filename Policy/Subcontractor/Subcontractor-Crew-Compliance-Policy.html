Policy Requirements

Each subcontractor / crew is required to follow all Cenvar Roofing policies in regard to workmanship, jobsite cleanliness and organization, uniforms, magnets, safety and professionalism. 

Please contact the Cenvar Roofing Safety Compliance Officer with questions regarding company safety policies: Nate Hertzog 434-435-0668 or safety@cenvarroofing.com

Violation of Cenvar Roofing Policies

Safety-related

For Subcontractor/Crew, the Subcontractor Safety Violation Citation shall be completed, and individual(s) charged based on the offenses listed in the form.

Non-safety related

Inspector is at their discretion to determine appropriate consequences for non-safety violations. The same form can be used that is included for safety-related offenses.

Subcontractor / Crew Compliance Bonus:

Crew members will receive a $100 bonus each time the crew member is named in a review (GuildQuality, Google, or CSS).
