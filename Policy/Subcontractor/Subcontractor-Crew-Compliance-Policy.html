Policy Requirements

Each subcontractor / crew is required to follow all Cenvar Roofing policies in regard to workmanship, jobsite cleanliness and organization, uniforms, magnets, safety and professionalism. 

Please contact the Cenvar Roofing Safety Compliance Officer with questions regarding company safety policies: Nate Hertzog 434-435-0668 or natehertzog@cenvarroofing.com

If a subcontractor / crew fails to maintain Cenvar Roofing policies:

  • The first occurrence will be a $250 fine.
  • The second occurrence will be a $500 fine.
  • The third occurrence will be 5 business days off.
  • Repeat offenses will result in loss of future work.

Subcontractor / Crew Compliance Bonus:

Beginning February 6, 2023, Subcontractor crews are able to earn a monthly Jobsite Compliance Bonus in the amounts of $750 for their English speaker and $500 for job site cleanliness (material, tools, and trash).

Crews will earn an additional $5 per square if they have an English speaker in a red shirt, and will still earn the additional monthly bonus if they pass the survey question.
