Various Call Center Policies:

When scheduling sales representative appointments, the daily appointment schedules must be filled as follows: Senior Sales Representative calendars get filled first, then they are to be filled based on individual Sales Representative conversion rates. 

  • Customer specific "day" preferences will also be taken into consideration.
  • Each week the Sales manager will review all sales data, and will discuss the appointment scheduling order with the Marketing Manager to instruct the Call Center.

For known repeat customers, the Call Center is to assign the customer’s original (if current) Sales Representative manually. If the customer would like to have a sooner appointment, the Call Center will schedule the first available Sales Representative.

Try to schedule an appointment when all the decision makers can be there.  If it is impossible to find an appointment where they can both be there, go ahead and schedule an appointment anyway.  

Always put into the notes on the calendar, who the salesperson is meeting, and whether they are the only decision maker.   That way the salesperson can attempt to reschedule later if he/she wants to.

When a branch sales (or repair) estimate schedule becomes full to one week, it is required that the Customer Call Center Representative create a post in the branch specific General Manager Google channel that there are no more available sales (or repair) appointments available for the next seven days,14 days, 21 days, etc.

Call Center Response: There are many variables in determining the price for each individual roof. In order to provide you an accurate price, we can schedule an estimator to come to your house at no charge to you, and provide you an estimate for your roof. If it is an inconvenience for you to meet with an estimator in person, we are able to have the estimator come to your house without you present to provide you the price. However, it is better if you are able to be there with the estimator, as there are many things that can affect the price of the roof.

Replacement and Repairs Appointments:

If the customer is unsure if they are in need of a roof replacement or roof repair, it is required to schedule the appointment with whoever (sales or repairs) is able to get to the customer the fastest, based on the current appointment load.

If a repairs estimator can get to the customer first and it's determined that a replacement is what is needed:

  1. The repairs estimator is required to get the measurements and if time allows a sales presentation.
  2. If time does not allow, the repairs estimator will send a proposal to the customer via email (a sales presentation is not required) and a follow-up call is required.

If a sales representative can get to the customer first and it's determined that a repair is needed:

  1. Shingles: The sales representative is required to figure out how to estimate the shingle repairs as needed.
    • Use all resources as needed (call manager/repairs manager for assistance).
  2. Metal: If a metal repair is needed, the sales representative will advise the customer to contact the Cenvar Roofing Call Center to schedule a metal repair estimate with the Repair team.

Existing Archived Customer: New Appointment

When an archived existing customer with an outdated sales estimate / proposal requests a new appointment to meet with a Sales Representative for an updated presentation and also to receive a refreshed estimate / proposal:

CC/OM: Unarchive the customer

CC/OM: Add a new appointment in the same job.

CC/OM: Move the file back to Estimate Appointment Scheduled (no matter where the file was previously located).

If the customer only requests an updated estimate / proposal that does not require an appointment:

CC/OM: Unarchive the customer.

CC/OM: Create a new Follow Up Reminder (not task) assigned to the sales representative to create an updated sales estimate / proposal.

CC/OM: The Follow Up Reminder should have a due date that is 3 business days in the future.

CC/OM: The file must remain in the same job stage, and should not be moved.

Temporary Tarps or Roof Inspection Appointments

If the customer would like to schedule a sales representative to perform a roof inspection or have a temporary tarp installed on their roof:

  • Call Center/Office Manager (CC/OM): The customer calls to request a roof inspection or a temporary tarping service.
  • CC/OM: Add a new customer in Job Progress. Entering A New Customer & Scheduling An Appointment
  • CC/OM: Add a new job in Job Progress. Entering A New Customer & Scheduling An Appointment
  • CC/OM: Make detailed notes in Job Progress specifying any details and/ or services the customer is requesting.

Note: Roof inspections will be performed for $350 and will be accompanied with a written inspection report (realtors, apartment complexes, multi-family buildings, commercial, etc. are the most common applications for this paid service). We do not perform free verbal roof inspections.

  • CC/OM: If applicable, notify the customer the cost of a roof inspection/temporary tarp installed is $350, and you will create a “Service Proposal” to be immediately emailed directly to the email which they provided. The customer will be required to sign the Service Proposal document electronically which will automatically be recorded in Job Progress.
  • CC/OM: Advise the customer that once the signed Service Proposal is received, the appointment will be scheduled.
  • CC/OM: Create the Service Proposal in Job Progress
    1. Check the box to indicate Temporary Tarping Service if applicable.
    2. Check the box to indicate Roof Inspection if applicable.
  • CC/OM: Email the Service Proposal to the customer.

STOP: Do not schedule the actual appointment until the signed Service Proposal is received from the customer.

  • CC/OM: Schedule the appointment for a time that works best for the customer. Scheduling A Sales Appointment
    1. **Temporary Tarp: only schedule in the next available repair estimate appointment time slot.
    2. **Roof Inspection: may be scheduled in the next available sales or repairs appointment time slot.

Policy on home buyers and Realtors representing home buyers:

Question: Will we schedule an estimate or a roof inspection for them?

Answer: Yes, we will schedule an inspection for a buyer, or a buyer’s Realtor, if either the buyer’s or seller’s Realtor meets us on site, or if we get confirmation directly from the seller or the seller’s Realtor that we are cleared to come at the specified time (the reason we will not accept the buyer’s Realtor scheduling with no meeting or confirmation is that we have had too many homeowners upset at us when we show up and they were not expecting us).

Question: Will we do a free estimate or inspection for a home buyer?

Answer: No. We do not perform free roof inspections. If the home is under contract with contingencies, we will still do an estimate or an inspection, but there will be a $350 charge. The charge will be credited back if the buyer closes on the house and elects to use us to do the work.

Cancelling a Sales Appointment

If the customer would like to cancel a scheduled sales appointment for roofing replacement, roofing repairs, roof tune up, or temporary tarp / roof inspection:

  • CC/OM: Change the work type to "Other".
  • CC/OM: Change the job Type to "Estimate Appointment Cancelled.
  • CC/OM: Delete the sales appointment and press the blue "proceed" button.
