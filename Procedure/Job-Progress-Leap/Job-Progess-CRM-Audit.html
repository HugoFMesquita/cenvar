What is the Job Progress (CRM) Audit?

The Job Progress Audit is a tool used by our Corporate Finance team to view randomly selected jobs and check that key fields are accurately filled in.

The General Manager receives the Job Progress Audit results each month for the employees in their branch.

The Job Progress Audit is used as part of each employee's yearly performance evaluation.

Which job positions are included in the CRM audit?

Sales Representatives

Office Managers

Project Coordinator

How are the jobs selected in Job Progress for the CRM audit?

Each month the accounting department will randomly select 1 job for each employee on the 6th, and 1 job for each employee on the 20th. (Sales Representatives will have additional jobs, see below)

Office Manager: 2 jobs per employee, per month total

Project Coordinator: 2 jobs per employee, per month total

Sales Representative: 

  • November 2020: 2 jobs per employee, per month total
  • December 2020 (and all future audits): 2 jobs per employee every 2 weeks, for a total of 4 jobs per month.
    • 2 jobs selected from the beginning CRM stages: Estimate Appt Scheduled thru Signed Contract.
    • 2 jobs selected from the ending CRM stages: Scheduled thru Warranteed and Closed.

Which Job Progress files will be audited?

All audits will be performed on customers that have had jobs which started beginning November 1, 2020.

Any customers with jobs starting prior to November 1, 2020 will not be audited.

How to do well on your Job Progress Audit?

Fill out all Job Progress job information daily. 

Fill out every required field in Job Progress without skipping over items.

Upload the required pictures and sheets pertinent to the job as it happens. Don't wait for later, do it while it is fresh on your mind.

Sales Representatives & Office Managers: Move the job into the correct bucket.  If you have followed up with a customer, move the job to the next bucket or stage.  The customer may need to be archived if it is a dead lead.

Job Progress (CRM) Audit FAQs:

  • Can I look at the the Job Progress Audit? Yes, absolutely! Here is a tutorial that shows how you can navigate the Job Progress Audit.
  • I'm a Project Coordinator, and I would like to know how the auditor decides what pictures are needed for a specific job? The auditor (and the Project Coordinator) will look at the proposal to see what the proposal specifies, and the required pictures will be based off that. It does NOT have to do what is checked/not checked on the PC checklist.
  • How can you tell whether a job is placed in the correct Job Progress stage? The auditor follows the job in Job Progress from the beginning stages all the way through to the signed contract. If the job is in "estimate appointment scheduled" stage, but has a proposal in that job, the audit will be marked as not being in the correct stage. If the job is in "estimated: sales f/u required" and there is a signed proposal in that job, the audit will be marked for not being in the correct stage.
  • Why do I need to add notes to the estimate? When we put notes on the estimate, they will automatically transfer to the work order. This way we are not possibly forgetting anything by the time we would have to fill the work order  out.
  • Does the customer need to actually sign the proposal, or is it sufficient that they 'accept the proposal' in Job Progress?  There have been instances where the customer has 'accepted the proposal', but did not sign the contract due to an old computer, phone, etc. We need a signed copy of the contract every time because in cases of non-payment, we have nothing to show that they agreed to pay for our services. In these types of scenarios please upload a paper copy of the proposal.
  • How can I rename a photo in Job Progress from my cell phone? Follow the below video tutorial showing how to rename a photo in the app. From photos, hold down the image and the rename option will pop up.
