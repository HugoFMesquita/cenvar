Use the search magnifying glass to search for the correct customer

Choose the customer

Highlight the jobs tab, then click on the job number for that customer

Depending on where the document would need to be located, choose the appropriate tab such as measurements, estimating, or forms and proposals

Select create

Choose the form/proposal template

Choose the document you would like to fill out

Fill out all required information, then save and rename the document appropriately

The completed document will now be located in the selected folder. 

Changes can be made by holding down the document. 

A window will pop up with an option to edit
