There will be one Corporate Customer Service Representative (CSR) team member assigned to make customer follow up calls.

  • The Customer Service Representative (CSR) will make all follow up calls after the job is completed, and after all known issues have been resolved.
    • CSR: will call customers on Wednesday following the week of job completion.
    • All jobs with problems are required to be in the Invoiced: Job Problems bucket in Job Progress to ensure the CSR does not contact for a follow up.
    • The branch is responsible to move all problem jobs into the Job Progress "Invoiced: Job Problems” bucket each week by close of business Tuesday.
  • The Customer Service Representative (CSR) will use the Customer Follow Up Call telephone questionnaire script.
  • The Customer Service Representative (CSR) is responsible to make a follow up phone call for one ”lost job” per salesman each month to ask specific questions about their experience.
