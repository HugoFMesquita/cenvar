The Cenvar Roofing Inventory Management Sheet is critical to ensure that all product & materials are accounted for daily.

Please also review the Material Distribution / Material Delivery Wiki for additional information.

It is the responsibility of the Office Manager to monitor and update both Inventory Management sheet tabs for their branch:

  • Branch Inventory Tab
  • Branch Jobs Tab

Once the Sales Representative sells a job, they are required to write the following on the Soft Schedule board in their branch:

  • Customer Name
  • Color of the Shingle (if selected at the time of signing)
  • Any time that the customer selects the shingle color, the Sales Rep is required to update the board with the color.
  • Number of Square

It is the responsibility of the Office Manager to monitor the Soft Schedule board for their branch.

  • Any new name will NOT have a 'checkmark' next to the name.
    • Note: A customer name that does not have a 'checkmark' will not be in the Inventory Management sheet.
  • All new names must be added to the Inventory Management sheet by close of business (COB) or before the OM leaves for the day.

 

Jobs Tab

The Office Manager (OM) will open the Inventory Management Sheet, and select the appropriate branch job tab located at the bottom of the sheet: Inventory Management Sheet

  • OM: daily analyze and determine which (if any) names on the board have not yet been entered in the Inventory Management Sheet.
  • OM: enter the new customer name into the Inventory Management sheet.
  • OM: add a 'checkmark' next to the customer name on the board.
    • Note: once the checkmark is added, ALL names should now have a checkmark indicating that they are all recorded in the Inventory Management sheet.

 

  • OM: enter the following information in the appropriate field:
    • Customer last name
    • # of square
    • # of cap bundles
    • Sold date of job (this is the date the proposal was signed)
    • Projected start date (6Wks out)
  • OM: send the material list via email to ABC to determine if they are able to fulfil the order.
  • OM: select the appropriate supplier from the dropdown menu:
    • ABC or Shop
    • select ABC is they will be fulfilling the order.
    • select 'shop' if Cenvar Roofing will be delivering the order, if ABC is unable to fulfil the materials.
  • OM: select 'YES' from the 'Sent' dropdown menu.
    • Only select Yes if the materials have actually left the facility, and are being delivered to a customer's address that day.

Inventory Tab

The Office Manager (OM) will open the Inventory Management Sheet, and select the appropriate inventory tab located at the bottom of the sheet: Inventory Management Sheet

  • Any time that shingles come IN or OUT of the branch, it must be accounted for, and accurately documented in this sheet.
  • It is the OMâ€™s responsibility to know what shingle quantities are currently on the yard.
  • OM: select the appropriate category from the dropdown menu:
    • Received: shingles were delivered or returned
    • Picked: shingles were shipped out for a job
    • Counts: cycle counts were performed by Corporate (Blake/Chris)
  • OM: will enter a separate line item for each 'transaction' that occurs each day.
    • The numbers are SQUARES (not bundles).
    • Location field:
      • Which job did it go to? Jones
      • Which supplier did it come from? Lowes
    • The date is the actual date of the shingle transaction.
  • The On Hand line will not change. These numbers reflect the initial cycle count.
  • OM: If adding materials back into stock:
    • a new line must be added for each material.
    • enter the item as a negative (-) to add it back into stock
      • example: "Received -3 "
