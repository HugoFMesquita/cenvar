CRM Audit: How to get a perfect score on the Sales Representative Audit. 

This tutorial will help you check each area of your work in Job Progress to ensure in a perfect audit score.

The auditors are looking at all of the following checkpoints when auditing Job Progress files:

Is the total job Price added to the Job Financial Summary?

  • The auditors look for the job price to be filled in on the “Finance Tab”.
  • This needs to be complete by the time the estimate is complete.
  • This assigns the Job a $ value and is visible in all the buckets.

Is the Estimate filed in the “Estimating” Folder?

  • The auditors check if the estimate doc is uploaded in the estimate folder.

Is the Subcontractor Labor calculated correctly to equal the amount of shingles in line #1 of the estimate sheet?

  • The auditors check that the labor matches the amount of shingles.
  • There can be a 1 bundle variance.

Is the Days of Labor field filled?

  • The auditor checks to make sure the field is filed for the job.

Is a picture uploaded that shows any relevant roof conditions in the photos folder?

  • The auditors check for any type of picture that may be found to be relevant during the sales estimate.
  • The before picture of the front of the house is a required picture.

Is a picture of the bid sheet uploaded in the photos folder?

  • The Auditors look for a picture of the bid sheet. 

Is the Signed Proposal in the Forms / Proposals folder signed by both the contractor & customer?

  • The proposal must be signed by both the salesperson & customer.

Is the Customer information entered correctly and matching the estimate?

  • The auditor checks that all customer info matches what is on the estimate.

Is the Job Duration days filled?

  • The auditor checks the number of days are in the proposal.

Are all “optional upgrades” checked yes or no on the proposal and is the price difference calculated correctly in the Grand Total?

  • The auditors check if these boxes are checked.
  • If not, no credit is given.
  • The example below would be given no credit.
  • The auditors also check the Grand total to be filled in on line 12.

Is a paper proposal / contract signed and uploaded to the Forms / Proposals folder if applicable?

  • If a signed paper proposal was done, it must be uploaded to the forms/ proposals folder

Is the Material List in the Materials folder?

  • The auditor checks to make sure there is a material list for the job in the the materials folder.

Is the Bring From Shop Material List in the Materials folder? 

  • The auditor checks if the shop pull list is in the materials folder.

Is the Work Order in the Work Orders folder?

  • The auditors check if the work order is in the work orders folder.

 

Are job specific notes added in Notes on the work order (if applicable)?

  • The auditors check for notes on the work order in the highlighted section below.
  • If you notate the estimate, it will transfer over to the work order .

 

Is an appropriate Job Flag selected for the customer’s experience? (upset customer, happy customer, etc)

  • The flag that applies to the scenario is selected for the customer situation.
  • If the job is complete, but there are issues, the auditors will check for a “Jobs complete but w/ issues” flag instead.
