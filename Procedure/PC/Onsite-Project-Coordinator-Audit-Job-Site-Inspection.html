The Cenvar Roofing Onsite Project Coordinator Audit / Job Site Inspection was created to ensure that all Project Coordinator policies & procedures are being followed.

Onsite Project Coordinator Audit

The Onsite Project Coordinator Audit is broken into 2 sections to check for all items on both Project Coordinator checklists:

  • Project Coordinator Truck Stocking Checklist
  • Project Manager Job Site Checklist (in Job Progress)

Who is included in the Onsite Project Coordinator Audit?

  • All Project Coordinators.

How are the jobs sites selected for the Onsite Project Coordinator Audit?

  • The job sites will be chosen at random.

How many jobs will be audited? And how often?

  • 1 job will be chosen per Project Coordinator per month.

Who will perform the Onsite Project Coordinator Audit?

  • General Managers, Regional Managers, Assistant General Managers, and Corporate Executives are all authorized to perform the PC job site inspections.

I have a question about the Onsite Project Coordinator Audit. Who can I talk to about my audit results?

  • Contact your branch General Manager.
  • Also, if something in the audit is unreasonable or you think is unnecessary, challenge it.  As always we welcome all feedback to help improve the process.
