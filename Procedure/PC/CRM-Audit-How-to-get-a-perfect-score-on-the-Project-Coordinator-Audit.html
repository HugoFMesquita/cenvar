The auditors are looking at all of the following checkpoints when auditing Job Progress files

Is the "boards used" field filled in?

The auditors look for a note in the job notes section that details the accurate and correct number of boards used on each specific job.

The auditors use this information to determine whether they need to score a before and after picture for bad wood in the audit.

Is there a "Roof before tear off" picture(s)?

This picture is only required if the salesperson does not upload a before picture of the house.

Is there a "Start of tear off" picture(s)?

The auditors look for a picture to show crew members tearing off shingles.

Is there an "Underlayment installed" picture(s)? 

The auditors look for a picture proving that underlayment was installed on the roof.

Is there a "Before picture of landscaping, patios, decks, driveway" picture(s)?

The auditors look for "before" pictures of the yard and any type of shrubbery around the house proving that the condition of the property is the same as when we first arrived.

Is there a "Bare roof deck" picture(s)?

The auditors look for a picture of the roof down to the bare wood.

Is there an "Ice and water around penetrations" picture(s)?

The auditors look for a picture that proves ice and water was installed in the valleys and any penetration point.

Is there a "Ridge vent cut out" picture(s)?

The auditors look for a picture to show that the ridge vent was cut out for proper ventilation.

This applies to both a roof with existing ridge vent and a roof where we add ridge vent.

 

Is there a "Last picture of the day" picture?

The auditors look for picture to be named as "Last Picture of the Day".

The picture must:

  • include any unfinished portion of the roof.
  • must be the last picture taken before you leave the jobsite.

Is the Safety checklist uploaded to the forms/proposals folder and signed by the subcontractor and the pc?

The auditors look for the safety checklist, and check that it was signed by both the PC and the subcontractor.

Is the project coordinator jobsite checklist uploaded to the forms/proposals folder?

The auditors look for the project coordinator checklist located in the forms and proposals folder.

Is there a picture(s) of the safety anchor?

The auditors look for a picture of the safety anchor.

  • Note: this photo is REQUIRED.

Is there a "Before and after of any wood replacement: picture(s)?

The auditors look for a picture of the wood replacement on the house.

There does not need to be a picture of every piece of wood replaced, but having several pictures is beneficial.

Is there a picture(s) of a collected referral coupon in the photos folder?

If applicable, the auditors look for a picture of the referral coupon given to you by the customer.

Is there a picture(s) of "Before and after of any metal work"? 

The auditors look for before and after pictures of metal on chimneys, masonry walls, etc.

Is there a picture(s) of cricket before and after if added?

The auditors look for pictures of the chimney before and after the cricket was added.
