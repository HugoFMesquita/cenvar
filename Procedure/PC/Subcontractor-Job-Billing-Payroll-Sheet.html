Subcontractor Job Billing / Payroll Sheet

Project Coordinators are required to fill out the current week’s payroll sheet once a job is completed in order for their subcontractor to be paid accurately. Specifically, the PC must daily fill out payroll by 12:00 pm (noon) the following day. Kyle Kayton (Regional Manager) will review all completed payroll daily. Example: Monday’s completed jobs will be reviewed by Wednesday.

The Subcontractor Job Billing / Payroll Sheet must be filled out in its entirety in order to complete the Project Coordinator Checklist. Project Coordinator Policies & Requirements

  • There are questions on the PC Checklist to determine if the Subcontractor Job Billing / Payroll Sheet has been completed.

Project Coordinator (PC): Open the Subcontractor Job Billing / Payroll Sheet for your branch.

  • Ask your branch General Manger (GM) if you do not have access.

PC: The first view is the 'Datasheet' tab.

  • Note the tab selected at the bottom of the Google Sheet.

Using the left/right arrows, select the correct Subcontractor (Sub) tab for the job.

  • NOTE: the first tabs are for General Manager use only.

PC: Fill out the Job Overview 

  • Notice fields specify either PC Entered or GM Entered
  • Only fill in fields for PC Entered data

 

  • NOTE: Each COLUMN is 1 (One) job, from top to bottom. (Not a row left to right!)

PC: Begin at the top of the column and fill in every field that is applicable to the job.

  • Job Overview
    • Use the dropdown menus to select PC, Salesman, & Subcontractor names.
    • Project Coordinators are required to add notes to Subcontractor Pay Sheet for all damages and deductions in the provided fields
  • Subcontractor Pay Detail
    • Fill in each material field with the appropriate values.
