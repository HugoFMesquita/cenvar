Once the customer is ready to Schedule Roof Job

Look up the desired customer in Job Progress, and make sure you have the Material list.

You need to know the SHINGLE COLOR & TRIM COLOR to determine the DRIP COLOR. 

  • if you do not have a drip edge color try to match it to the shingle.
  • if you have white trim, you should always match with white drip, brown with brown drip, and black with black drip.

Material list: enter the customer shingle color in the first row. 

Find the drip edge and enter the drip edge color (White, black or brown) 

At the bottom in the notes add: 

  • Shingle color
  • Drip edge color
  • Start Date
  • Delivery Date (For ABC SUPPLY)
  • Drop off instructions if given by customer.

Click SAVE, click the 3 horizontal dots, then click email to send to your local ABC Supply. 

  • If you need assistance with your local ABC Supply contact information, ask your General Manager.

Once the customer is scheduled, slide the job from SIGNED CONTRACT to SCHEDULED 

The day before the job begins, call and update the customer to tell them the time the crew will be arriving (give yourself a window - typically anytime between 7-8am).

  • Ask the customer if the material has been delivered.
  • Print and text a picture of the material list to the Project Coordinator.

The day of the job, move the job from SCHEDULED to WORK IN PROGRESS.

You should receive updates around 3-3:30 from the Project Coordinator via the branch job-updates Google chat channel.

  • The PC will let you know how much decking was replaced, if any was replaced, and if the crew is on schedule.
  • This call will prepare you for updating the next customer.

Receiving a Payment

See Receiving a Payment in Job Progress

The customer can pay via:

  • Cash
  • Check
  • Credit or debit card
    • Note: we charge a 3% credit card fee that you will add to the final invoice.

Check payment:

  • Locate the customer
  • Click FINANCIALS
  • Click the + at the top right corner of Payment Received (the box titled RECEIVE PAYMENT)
  • Enter the amount written on the check
  • Payment type (Check)
  • LINK check to invoice (Only link checks)
  • Cash and credit card Payments: YOU DO NOT LINK PAYMENT
  • SAVE payment
  • If paid in full and the job is under PAID, you can REGISTER ROOF WARRANTY.

Taking a Credit Card Payment

Credit Card Payment: ONLY IN QB then you add received payment in JP

  • Log into QB
  • Add the 3% fee before anything else
  • Tell the customer the final total amount including the 2% fee
  • Enter the amount in PAYMENT AMOUNT
  • Click credit debit
  • Enter card number, expiration date, 3-digit card security code
  • CLICK “Process payment”
  • Email or mail the customer a paid invoice

Steps to Register a Warranty

Pull up the customer in CRM 

Look over notes just in case you have additional information 

Log onto // https://login.owenscorning.com/users/sign_in

Sign in 

Click on Warranties, Register Warranties, Register a warranty 

Step 1. Click EXTENDED WARRANTY 

  • Request individual purchase clicks YES 
  • Click System for a system warranty only if the customer paid extra for platinum
  • Click platinum job 
    • The job*should* be flagged if the warranty needs to be registered as platinum
    • Always check the proposal to verify if the customer selected platinum 

Click next STEP 2

  • All the boxes with the red * need to be filled out 
  • Name, address, zip code, state, phone number 
  • *For preferred contact method click email and make sure your Cenvar email is populated in the email box above 
  • The box below Property Information-installation address 
  • If the billing address is different enter it if the property is the same from property owner check the box with “is the same as the property owner”  

Click next STEP 3

  • Enter property type, single family detach, other if different 
  • Pitch, date of installation, product, color, squares replaced 
  • Enter the OC products used (all this information will be on the MATERIAL LIST)
  • Check the box  

Step 4 Agree to terms and conditions 

  • Verify information / look over all the information make sure it is all correct and confirm once you confirm you will see and registration number pop up 
  • Copy and paste that onto the job notes in the customer account 
  • Click VIEW IN CART or REGISTER A NEW WARRANTY (If you have more to register) 
  • Once you check out, the actual warranty registration will pop up in your email. 
    • You will email it to the customer if you have their email.
    • If you do not have the customer email, print and send it via snail mail. 

NOTE: The OM will send one warranty email to the customer with all applicable product warranty information attached:

  • roof warranty
  • power fan warranty
  • skylight warranty

Standing seam

Give Max Kendall 2 weeks notice when scheduling a metal project.

Speak with your General Manager to determine the contact information of the metal location closest to your branch.
