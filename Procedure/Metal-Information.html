Cenvar Roofing Metal Installation specifics:

Cenvar installs metal rib panels ("screw down") and Snaplock ("standing seam")

Inside closure strips should be used for eaves only and universal closure strips everywhere else.

Standard screw down master rib panels can be installed down to 3/12 pitch using our standard installation practices.

1" profile Snaplock can be installed down to and including 3/12 pitch.

Snaplock jobs will no longer use nail strips but use 26g with a clip system to alleviate any "oil canning" issues. To determine the number of clips per job: Take the total square of the roof, multiply that number by 12 then divide by 16.

Low pitch guidelines for metal roofing:

  • 2/12 & 1/12 pitch requires the use of 1.75" Snaplock.
  • We do not install metal roofing under 1/12 pitch.
  • We do install ice and water shield over the entire facet of anything less than 4/12 pitch.

Cenvar Roofing Job Site Policy

 

Metal Labor & OP:

For metal roofs: labor is figured and paid on actual square footage.

The labor figure on the estimate, work order, and payroll should all be the actual square footage amount. 

  • The estimate macro in Job Progress reflects this.

Labor should be figured on actual square footage for master rib and standing seam.

OP should be figured on actual square footage for master rib and standing seam.

 

General Metal Guidelines:

Due to color variations and metal profiles not matching properly, we will not mix vendors for any metal jobs.

All 24-gauge roofing systems will be sold with 24-gauge panels and 24-gauge trim. 24-gauge roofing systems will no longer be sold with 29-gauge trim.
