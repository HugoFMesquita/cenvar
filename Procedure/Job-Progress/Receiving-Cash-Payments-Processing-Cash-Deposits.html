Customer Cash Payments

  1. Office Manager (OM): receive the cash payment in Job Progress.
  2. OM: notify the Corporate Finance Department within 24 hours of the cash receipt. (justin@cenvarroofing.com or mary@cenvarroofing.com)
    • Provide the following details (this information is necessary to reconcile the payment in QuickBooks):
      1. customer name
      2. date received
      3. amount received
  3. General Manager (GM) is authorized to take the cash payment(s) to the bank to deposit the cash payment(s) into the Cenvar Roofing bank account.
    • Corporate Finance Department (Justin/Mary) can provide the bank account number to include in the bank deposit slip.
    • All cash must be deposited within 1 week of receipt.
  4. The General Manager is also authorized to give the cash payment(s) directly to the Regional Manager during his weekly visit (if it is within 1 week of receipt).
    • The Regional Manager will deliver the cash payments to the Corporate Office.
  5. OM: email the deposit slip to the Corporate Finance Department after the deposit has been made. This is important for record keeping. Email to: justin@cenvarroofing.com
  6. GM must notify the Corporate Finance Department (Justin) if they would like to keep petty cash in the branch. Justin: justin@cenvarroofing.com
