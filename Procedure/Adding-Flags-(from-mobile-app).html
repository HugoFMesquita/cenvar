Job Flags

To add a job flag, follow these steps.

Open the Job Progress app and use the search button to search the customer.

Choose the customer that you want to add a flag to.

Select the jobs tab, then hold down the screen over the customer information box.

A quick actions box will pop up. Choose the job flags option.

Choose the appropriate job flag and click 'ok'.

The flag will now show up under the customer information when the job is selected.

Customer Flags

To add a customer flag, follow these steps.

Search the customer and select the jobs tab.

Click the three lines and choose edit a customer.

Choose add flags.

Select the correct customer flag and click 'ok'. The flag will be added to the customer and become searchable in Job Progress.
