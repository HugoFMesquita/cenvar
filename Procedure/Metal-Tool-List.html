All Repair team members must have the first 3 items on tool list purchased within 2 days of their start date. The additional items will be purchased by the General Manager, and are company property.

General Managers are responsible to have the Metal and EPDM tool lists on hand at the shop for the Repair team to use when they have a Metal or EPDM job.

If any tools are lost it is the responsibility of the person who used it to replace it with his/her personal funds.
