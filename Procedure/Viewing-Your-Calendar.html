On a computer

From the Job Progress Home screen, select Staff Calendar from drop down menu under Calendar:

A FILTERS menu will appear on the left side of the screen:

If your name is not displayed, click on the blue + MORE option. A new window will pop up to be able to search for the user name:

Type in part of the name until desired name appears. Placing a check beside the name will cause the calendar to appear.
