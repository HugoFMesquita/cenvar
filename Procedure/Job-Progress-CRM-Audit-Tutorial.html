Each month, the Cenvar Roofing accounting department randomly selects Job Progress files to check for user accuracy and user entries. This tutorial will help you navigate the CRM Audit.

Open the Job Progress CRM Audit Google Sheet:

CRM Audit Google Sheet

Locate the correct tab at the bottom of the sheet which corresponds to your current job position. In this example, we are using the Sales CRM Scoreboard.

Press the blue plus sign (+) located near your name. This will expand your personal data, and will allow the ability to view all files which were audited.

Expanded data:

Press the blue plus sign (+) located near each customer name to expand the data.
