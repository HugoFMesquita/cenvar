Customer Referrals

Many times an existing customer will refer a friend, neighbor, or family member to Cenvar Roofing for their roofing needs. We promise our customers that they will receive a $100 gift card for referrals; we must make sure we deliver on our promise.

Initial contact

While scheduling a new appointment, the Call Center asks the customer how they heard about Cenvar Roofing. If they indicate they were referred by an existing, previous, or current customer, the Call Center will select “Existing Customer” and enter the name of the customer in the drop down menu in Job Progress. If referrals are entered after the initial setup of the customer, the same steps should be followed.

Note: An additional field in Job Progress can be used to specify “Gift card is needed” along with using the “Referred, gift card needed” flag for an additional precaution, but should be able to be phased out.

See Scheduling a Sales Appointment - Customer Call Center (Lead Development), and Office Managers and Entering a New Customer & Scheduling an Appointment for additional information.

Reporting

 Finance/Accounting runs a report every Monday that shows customers that have made referrals and send it to the Call Center, similar to the example below.

Verification

The Call Center is then responsible for identifying customers who have satisfied the requirements for receiving their gift card:

  • Existing customer has paid in full; and
  • Referred customer has at least scheduled an appointment with Cenvar Roofing.

Send note with gift card

The Call Center will handwrite a Thank You card and enclose a $100 Visa gift card.

Customer Referrals

Many times an existing customer will refer a friend, neighbor, or family member to Cenvar Roofing for their roofing needs. While scheduling a new appointment, the Call Center will ask the customer which method they were referred for service. If referred by ‘Existing Customer’ is selected, the Call Center will enter the name of previous customer and will also make a note that a ‘Gift card is needed’ in the available field. 

  1. Add a NOTE in "Job Notes" in Job Progress with the following information:
    • The name of the existing customer who referred them who is to receive a $100.00 Visa gift card.
  2. Set the Job Flag, "Referred, Gift Card Needed".
  3. The Job Note / Job Flag will alert the Office Manager to create a line item on the customer's invoice for the referral coupon redemption.
  4. The Office Manager will notify the Corporate Lead Center to purchase a $100.00 Visa Gift Card to mail to the referring customer.

 

Visa Gift Cards (for referring customers):

Visa Gift Cards for referring customers are only approved to be purchased and sent by the Cenvar Roofing Corporate Call Center.

  • Office Manger: notify the Cenvar Roofing Corporate Call Center to purchase a $100.00 Visa Gift Card, and provide the Call Center with the referring customer's name & address.
  • Call Center Manager: purchase the $100.00 Visa gift card.
  • Call Center Manager: use a Cenvar Roofing note card to hand write a "Thank you from Cenvar Roofing" personalized message to send with the Visa gift card in the matching envelope.
  • Call Center Manager: mail the Visa gift card the same day it is received.
  • Note: the Visa gift card must be mailed within 5 business days of the invoice date.
