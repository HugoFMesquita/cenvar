Standard Workmanship Warranty

10 year workmanship warranty, with a $250.00 service charge to be added for each go-back diagnosis if Cenvar Roofing is not found to be at fault. Customer Initiated Go Back Procedure

  • Metal roofing workmanship warranty is 10 years for screw down
  • Metal roofing workmanship warranty is 10 years for standing seam.
  • “Layover” shingle jobs are only warranted for 3 years.
  • “Layover” screw-down jobs receive a 10 year workmanship warranty.

Upgraded Workmanship Warranty 

50 year workmanship warranty includes free leak diagnosis for 50 years, no matter who is at fault.

  • The cost for the Upgraded Workmanship Warranty is $15.00 per square (based on overage).
  • The 50 year workmanship warranty can be offered on any owner-occupied, detached, single-family home, on any product that we offer except for metal roofing.
    • Metal roofing workmanship warranty is 10 years for screw down
    • Metal roofing workmanship warranty is 10 years for standing seam.
  • Any 50 year workmanship warranty other than asphalt shingle (Divinci, Matterhorn, Etc) is to be priced at $20.00 per square (based on overage).
