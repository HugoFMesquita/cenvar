When you receive a telephone call that is for a different branch.

Kindly tell the customer that you are happy to transfer them to the desired branch.

Give the customer the desired branch direct telephone number in case they need it in the future or or in case you are disconnected.

There are 2 ways to transfer the phone call:

  1. Press the transfer button
    • Dial the direct telephone number or branch extension.
    • The phone call will automatically transfer.
  2. Place the customer on HOLD by pressing the 'HOLD' button. Note: while on the call, you will see buttons on the bottom left side of the phone screen for: 'HOLD', 'END CALL', 'TRANSFER', & 'MORE'.
    • Once the customer is on HOLD press 'TRANSFER'.
    • Key the desired branch extension number on the number pad.
    • Note: IF THE CALLER IS ON HOLD: WHEN YOU TRANSFER YOU MUST HANG UP THE LINE SO THAT THE OTHER OFFICE CAN TAKE THE CALL.

Where can I find my telephone extension?

Located on the right side of the telephone screen is a small phone icon with a green checkmark. The number located to the right of the small phone icon is your telephone extension.

Retrieving Voice Mails

A red light will flash on your desk telephone until you have 'checked' all voice messages.

The number of missed calls and unchecked messages will be displayed under the red light on the phone screen.

  • Note: The red light will continue to flash even if you have checked all of the voice messages, unless you delete the message after listening.

Press the button with the 'envelope' icon.

Enter your voice mail passcode. Example: "1234#".

  • Note: see you General Manger if you do not know the voice mail passcode.

Press 1 to access mailbox.

Listen to all messages while taking detailed notes on your notepad.

Press 7 to delete a message.

Press 2 to save a message.

Note: you will be able to press 2 to save the message once you listen to the full message.

  • Note: you will be able to pause the message midway by pressing 2.
