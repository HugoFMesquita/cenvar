The First Report of Injury form shall:

be completed by employees as soon as possible, and given to their manager for further action.

be filled out for all work related accidents & on the job injuries.

be turned into their manager no more than 24 hours after the injury.

If you are reporting an automobile accident, the Vehicle / Asset Accident Report Form should be completed in lieu of the First Report of Injury Form.

  • However, if you have been injured as a result of an auto accident, you are required to fill out both forms.

General Manager Responsibilities

Use the GM Job Injury Reporting Checklist to ensure all steps have been completed, including

  • Notify Human Resources within 1 hour of any injury incident or vehicle accident on the Human Resources Google Chat channel.
  • Send the completed First Report of Injury on the Human Resources Google Chat channel no more than 24 hours after the incident.

Note: Any time a company credit card is used to pay for medical treatment, General Managers are required to send all medical invoices, receipts, or bills . These are REQUIRED by our insurance company as proof of self payment in order to be in compliance with worker’s compensation claims. 

Before returning to work

Any time an employee is injured at home or at work, they are required to obtain a doctor’s note releasing them to return to work. Return To Work Policy

For work related injuries, employees are required to use an approved physician as per our Workers Compensation approved list: Workers Compensation
