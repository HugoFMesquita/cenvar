How to access Google Chat through Gmail (Desktop)

Click on the circled 9 menu dots in the top portion of your computer screen.

Click the Google “Chat” icon (circled below):

The left of your screen will display the Google chat "rooms" (Blue Box) and also the individual Google chat messages (Red Box). 

How to access Google Chat through Mobile (iPhone/iPad) 

Download the Google Chats App and log in with your Cenvar Roofing email account.

You are able to view individual chat messages (below) by selecting the 'Chat' button on the bottom.

You are able to view Google chat "rooms"(below) by selecting the 'Rooms' button on the bottom.
