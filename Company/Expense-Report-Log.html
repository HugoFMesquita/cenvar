Expense Log: All Cenvar Roofing employees with a Company Credit Card have access to the Expense Log. The following fields are required to be filled in by the card holder by EOB daily.

  • Payee
  • Account
  • Class
  • Note- If you do not see the account that your transaction would go in,  leave a detailed note of what the purchase is.
  • Expense Reports will be sent out to the General Managers on Mondays and Thursdays at 10:00am to allow sufficient time to class each expense.
