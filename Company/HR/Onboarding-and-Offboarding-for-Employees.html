Onboarding for Employees

General Manager Responsibility: When a new employee is hired, the New Hire Form must be filled out and emailed to HR@cenvarroofing.com. All new employees are required to report to Cenvar Roofing Corporate Offices at 10:00am on their first day of employment for orientation. The General Manager will schedule the orientation appointment with Human Resources: HR@cenvarroofing.com
