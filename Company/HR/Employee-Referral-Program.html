The Cenvar family of companies (Cenvar Roofing, Cenvar Solar, Ravnec, and Varcen) desires to attract and retain high quality employees who exemplify our core values of grit, servanthood, efficiency, positivity, integrity, growth, and self-discipline. Referrals by current employees are highly encouraged.

The Employee Referral Program will pay $1500 for any referral given by a current employee that results in a hire. The referral bonus will be paid after the referred employee has been employed for at least 6 months.

If you have any questions regarding the Employee Referral Program, please contact Human Resources at HR@cenvarroofing.com
