Cenvar Roofing Job Offer Template

The new employee’s manager must fill out the appropriate job offer template, and have the Regional Manager (Branch) or Human Resource Manager (Corporate) sign before giving it to the employee to sign.

  • Branch: The Regional Manager must approve and sign off on all job offers BEFORE it is presented to the employee.
    • All official job offers must be signed by the Regional Manager, General Manager, and the Employee. The Regional Manager must sign off on the job offer before it is presented to the employee
  • Corporate: The Human Resource Manager must sign off on all job offers BEFORE it is presented to the employee.
