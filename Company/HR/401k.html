Cenvar Roofing 401K

Where can I get information about 401k? You will be given a 401K booklet at the start of employment with Cenvar Roofing.

How do I sign up for 401K? Employees can contact Principal if they would like to sign up for the 401k plan. The Cenvar Roofing Human Resource department registers each new employee with Principal (regardless of whether or not they join).
