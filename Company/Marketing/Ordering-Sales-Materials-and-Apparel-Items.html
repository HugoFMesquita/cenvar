It is the responsibility of each General Manger to order apparel for the employees in their branch.

To order hats, t- shirts, hoodies and vests, General Managers will place the order on the Google Chat Distribution & Assets channel.

To order all other apparel, such as polos, jackets, etc., General managers will place the order through the Cenvar Roofing Marketing Department. Please contact Stephen Vernon directly: stephen@cenvarroofing.com

It is the responsibility of the Sales Manager to monitor all all branch sales materials, and will order as need to keep the branch fully stocked.

Please contact Allen Branscome directly for any questions: allen@cenvarroofing.com

The Sales Manager will assign a sales representative at each branch to monitor the sales material inventory for their branch, and will notify the Sales Manager when items are in need of reordering.
