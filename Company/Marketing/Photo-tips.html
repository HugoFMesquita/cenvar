Photo Tips for Marketing Images

Images are powerful tools - they are memorable, communicate faster than words, keep readers engaged, and help drive sales. This wiki provides helpful tips when taking photos to maximize their effectiveness. 

Examples provided are directed toward Cenvar Solar, but the same principles apply to the entire Cenvar brand of companies including Varcen.

Corporate Office

1. Entrance and Logo: 

Capture the entrance with the Cenvar's name and logo prominently displayed. This will help with brand recognition. 

2. Employee Interaction: 

Show employees in their work environment, highlighting teamwork, discussions, or any activity that embodies culture. 

3. Eco-Friendly Features: 

If the office has sustainable or green features, be sure to photograph them. This reinforces commitment to sustainability. 

Job Sites 

1. Employees at Work: 

Capture employees installing solar panels or equipment. It showcases expertise and commitment. ALWAYS make sure to capture Cenvar's brand. Example: Cenvar worker with Cenvar apparel working on a roof, finished photo with a yard sign/Cenvar vehicle parked in the street with an overall shot of the home or just the logo in the top corner.

 2. Equipment: 

Detailed shots of solar panels, inverters, or other machinery that is essential to the job. 

3. Drones: 

If possible, aerial shots can provide a unique perspective of larger installations. 

Recent Projects 

1. Homeowner: 

If homeowners are willing, capture them with the new solar installations, capture Cenvar's name and logo in the shot. This can be a powerful marketing tool. 

2. Golden Hour: 

Take photos during the golden hour (shortly after sunrise or before sunset) to make the solar installation stand out with the warm light. Again, capture Cenvar's name and logo in the shot! 

3. Technical Details: 

Close-ups of junction boxes, mounts, or unique installation methods that set Cenvar apart. 

4. Integration with Home Design: 

Show how solar panels not only serve a functional purpose but also blend seamlessly with a home's aesthetics. 

General Tips 

1. Consistent Branding: 

ALWAYS attempt to incorporate Cenvar's branding elements into the pictures. 

2. Storytelling: 

Craft a narrative with photos, or video. This could be the journey of a solar installation project from start to finish or the day in the life of a technician. This would be great for a blog article, or a YouTube video! 

3. Editing: 

Enhance clarity, brightness, and sharpness of photos. 

The aim is to not only showcase Cenvar's brand, but also our values, dedication to sustainability, and commitment to the community.
