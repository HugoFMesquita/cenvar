  • Note: Roof inspections will be performed free-of-charge to homeowners of single-family homes, solar companies, & any company that owns the structure they wish to inspect and will include an oral report. All other roof inspections will be performed for $250 and will be accompanied with a written inspection report (realtors, apartment complexes, multi-family buildings, commercial, etc.. are the most common applications for this paid service). A homeowner of a single-family home can still request a written report, but they will be charged $250.

Instructions for Completing form

1. Open Job Progress, desktop or app.

2. Search for and select the desired customer job.

3. Click Forms/Proposals located in the menu on the left.

4. Click the blue Choose Template button.

5. Select the Roof Inspection Form Template.

6. Click the blue Create button.

7. Perform the roof inspection.

  • Note: if you notice an area is not listed on the Inspection Form, contact Aimee Camm - 434-849-7297.

8. Use the drop-down menu to select the appropriate condition for each line item.

  • G: Good, No Action.
  • F: Fair.
  • P: Poor, Immediate Action.
  • Not Accessible if applicable

9. Press the blue Update button when finished.

10. Make detailed notes as needed.

11. Press the blue Update button.

12. Type in any detailed inspection notes in the area provided.

13. Sign the Inspection Report.

14. Press the blue Save button in the top right.

15. Name the Inspection Report.

16. Press the blue Save button.

17. Email the Inspection Report to the customer.

18. Move the job to the Work In Progress stage in Job Progress.

  • This will notify the Office Manager to create an invoice for the service.
  • The OM daily reviews this job stage.
