The Cenvar Roofing Roof Inspection Form is to be used for roof inspections. Please refer to the Job Progress Service Proposal – Temporary Tarps or Roof Inspections Process Wiki for additional information.

  • Note: Roof inspections will be performed free-of-charge to homeowners of single-family homes, solar companies, & any company that owns the structure they wish to inspect, and will include an oral report. All other roof inspections will be performed for $250 and will be accompanied with a written inspection report (realtors, apartment complexes, multi-family buildings, commercial, etc. are the most common applications for this paid service) A homeowner of a single-family home can still request a written report, but they will be charged $250.

Completing form

Open Job Progress.

  • Desktop or App

Search for & select the desired customer job.

Click Forms/Proposals located in the menu on the left.

Click the blue "Choose Template" button.

Select the Roof Inspection Form Template.

Click the blue "Create" button.

Perform the roof inspection.

  • Note: if you notice an area is not listed on the Inspection Form, contact Aimee Camm - 434-849-7297.

Use the dropdown menu to select the appropriate condition for each line item.

Use the dropdown menu to select the appropriate condition for each line item.

  • Mark: F- Fair.
  • Mark: P- Poor, Immediate Action.
  • Mark: Not Accessible if applicable

Press the blue "Update" button when finished.

 

Make detailed notes as needed.

Sign the Inspection Report.

Press the blue "Save" button in the top right.
