Scary Collection Letter

Before printing the document, you need to type today's date at the top of the letter, and also change the 'failure to pay by' date in the body of the letter to 30 days in the future.

Example:

Date: (type today's date here)
Dear Cenvar Roofing customer,

Our records indicate that your invoice is 30+ days past due. A statement of your invoice and finance charge(s) is attached.

Please refer to Section 10. Cost of Collections and Section 14. Payment Terms and Agreement of the contract. Failure to pay by December 31, 2020 (change date here) will result in your debt being turned over to our attorney.

Sincerely,

Cenvar Roofing
