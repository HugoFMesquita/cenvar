Cenvar Roofing Company History

As a boy, Chris Good began to help in his father's company and learned how to build decks, house additions, and roofing.

1997: Chris branched out on his own as Good's Construction Company LLC, and obtained his Class B Construction license to build decks & additions.

1999: Good's Construction Company obtained a Class A Construction license and started to build houses.

2000s: Good’s Construction Company specialized in building large, high end custom homes.

2008: U.S. housing market crisis affected the home building industry which caused Goods Construction Company to scale back and only build smaller homes.

2010: Chris Good began to purchase foreclosed houses and hired a team to help rehab the foreclosures.

2012: He partnered with Juan Villegas and placed an ad in the paper to start the company. Good’s Construction Company started specializing in residential re-roofing and officially became Lynchburg Roofing.

2014: Lynchburg Roofing purchased the current property on Crowell Lane.

2016: Roanoke (Vinton), VA, branch opened.

2017: Charlottesville, VA, branch opened.

2019: Richmond, VA, branch opened.

2019: Lynchburg Roofing held a regional contest to rename the company with a $25,000 grand prize. The winning name was: Cenvar Roofing. Cenvar is a blend of words paying homage to “Central Virginia.” The 2nd prize prize was awarded for getting the most people to enter the contest of $10,000.

2019: New River Valley, VA, branch opened.

2020: Fredericksburg, VA, branch opened.

2021: Farmville, VA, branch opened.

2021: Harrisonburg, VA branch opened.
