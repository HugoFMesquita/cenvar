The Cenvar Roofing Step It Up Challenge is a fun group challenge that involves using a pedometer, activity tracker or smart phone pedometer app to count your daily steps. The Cenvar Roofing employee with the most steps at the end of the challenge wins! Counting our daily steps can help us to become more aware of our physical activity habits, and can help with our overall heart health.

The Cenvar Roofing 21 Day Workout Challenge is a is a commitment to making physical and fitness health a priority by moving your body for 30 minutes each day for the 21 days in efforts to form a new healthy habit. That is it! 

February 2022 Cenvar Fit Events: Cenvar employees were challenged to get outside and play Disc Golf or run a 5K in the month of February.
