Customer Cash Payments

Cash payments include legal tender and any form of check (personal, certified, etc.).

Any cash payments for Cenvar Solar shall be taken to the Solar COO or Solar CGO within 1 week of acceptance. The Solar COO/CGO shall ensure cash payments are received by HR/Finance at Corporate.

HR/Finance shall receive payment into QuickBooks within 24 hours. Cashier's checks shall be deposited in the bank within 1-2 business days.
