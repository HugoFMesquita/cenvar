Cenvar Solar offers financing through Sunlight and Atmos.

Customers who opt to use financing go through several phases:

  1. Pre-approval - This initial phase determines the likelihood of the customer being approved for financing.
  2. Selection of rate/loan options and submit for approval.
  3. Receive 100% approval if qualified.

Financed Sales are not considered “sold” until the customer has completed all three steps. Operations shall not begin welcome calls until the customer has been fully approved.
