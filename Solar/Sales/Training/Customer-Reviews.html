Conducting a Customer Review

Congratulate them on their new Solar System

Ask them if they are willing to leave a review on their experience.

Ask them if there are any reasons that they could not give us a 5-star review.

  • Listen to them and see if there is anything we need to do to make it right so they could give us a 5-star review.

How to send the customer a link for a review

Log into the Chekkit website or App.

  • If you do not have access, contact Stephen Vernon.

Enter the Customers, name, or email in the center box.

Click Review Invitation

  • You can Edit the wording within the email before you send it.
  • For any customers that are outside of Lynchburg you may want to remove the word Lynchburg. Do not add any other branch location.

Click send.

A link to Google and Solar Reviews will go to the customer for them to leave a review on Cenvar Solar.
