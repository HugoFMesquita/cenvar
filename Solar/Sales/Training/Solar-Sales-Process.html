Tell them about Cenvar Solar

  1. Cenvar has worked for more than a decade to establish a name synonymous with trust. We’re proud to bring that same trust to the solar industry. As with roofing, we provide our customers with the best products, services, and warranties available.
  2. Solar Energy is about saving money.
  3. Cenvar’s goal is to meet your needs by providing you with a quality product at a reasonable price and servicing you through the life of the system.

Ask questions to identify their interest in Solar.

  1. Year the house was built.
  2. Type of roof.
  3. Age of the roof.
  4. Any obstructions that need to be removed to increase their solar productivity?
  5. Why are they interested in adding solar? Reduce monthly Electricity cost, environment, ROI.
  6. What concerns do they have about adding solar?
  7. What is their current level of interest in solar? Gathering information, looking to get better educated, ready for a purchase?
  8. Have you investigated Solar?
  9. What do they know about solar?
  10. On a scale of 1-5 what is their likelihood to go Solar? (1= No, 5 = Yes).

Ideas for why to go Solar.

  1. Shield yourself from rising electric rates.
  2. 30% federal Tax Credits
  3. SRECs
  4. Own your energy production.

Warranty

Cenvar's Guarantee

Output will be 100% of what we have stated, or we will add panels to get to 100% for free. If panels cannot be added, we will pay a rebate for the percentage difference of the guarantee vs. what it is producing plus 2%.

Follow Up

  1. Ask permission from customer to follow up with them.
  2. Ask customer the best method of communication to reach them at (call, email, text).
  3. If you call them, leave a voicemail if they do not answer.
  4. Set a time with the customer for the next follow up and create a Task or Meeting in HubSpot.
  5. Continue to follow up with them until the sign a contract or give a firm no.

Checklist once contract is signed

  1. In Aurora: if there are multiple designs, append the name of the final design by adding the word FINAL to the beginning of the sold design name.
  2. In HubSpot: if there are multiple proposals in attachments, do the following steps:
    • Append the name of the proposal the customer chooses by adding the word FINAL to the beginning of the current proposal and save in your folder on the Google drive with all proposals.
    • Upload this proposal into HubSpot under the Deal name in attachments.
  3. In HubSpot, move the deal into the Signed Contract stage.
  4. If the Customer is waiting on lender approval, move the deal into the Lender Approval stage.
  5. Once Customer has lender approval, move the deal into the Signed Contract stage.
  6. If the deal is a referral, complete the information within the Referral section on the left sidebar of the deal.
