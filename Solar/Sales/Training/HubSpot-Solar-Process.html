Deals 

Sales Pipeline

  • The customer will go through the sales pipeline stages until they have signed a contract or DQ/Lost.

Operations Pipeline

  • Once the customer has been moved into the Signed Contract, they enter the Operation Pipeline and follow the Operations Pipeline Stages. 

Sales Pipeline Stages:

New Lead

For Customer’s responding to a marketing ad, the customer is setup by the Lead Center under Deals, Sales Pipeline as a New Lead

The Lead Center Rep contacts the customer to set up appointment with a Sales Specialist

  • Once an appointment (In-person or virtual) is set the deal is moved to the Appointment Set.
  • Appointment shows in HubSpot, and meeting invite is emailed to Sales Specialist and Customer as a Google meeting invite for the day and time of the appointment.

Sales Specialist should review the following information in HubSpot:

  • Name, address, email are all present
  • Review the notes.
  • Listen to the initial call from the Lead Center to better understand the customer.

For Self-Generated Leads the Sales Specialist needs to enter the Deal into HubSpot. When the new deal is set up the Sales Specialist should put the deal into the appropriate stage. Create a New Deal

Call Attempt

A call has been made to set up an appointment with the customer and customer did not answer the call.

The Lead Center will continue to contact this person.

Appointment Set

Sales Specialist has the appointment (Virtual or In-Person)

  • Email sent to customer and Sales Specialist to show up on Google calendar. Appointment will show as in-home or virtual. If virtual, a link to Google hangout is in the meeting request.
  • Enter notes from the appointment into the Note Field
  • Schedule a follow up with the customer either as a meeting or task.
  • Solar Specialist Appointment Rules

Proposal Issued

If a proposal is issued to the customer, move the deal to the Proposal Issued. Enter the following from the Drop-down menu.

  • Dollar Amount of system
  • System size in watts
  • Inverter Type
  • # of Solar Panels
  • Panel Manufacture
  • Payment Type
  • Deposit Amount
  • Finance Company
  • Estimated 1st Year Production
  • Adders
  • Contract Notes
  • Attach the proposal(s) in HubSpot in the right-side bar under
  • Attachments.
    • If you email the proposal to the customer through HubSpot the proposal will show up in the attachments.
  • If a proposal was not issued move the contact to either Follow Up, DQ, or Lost depending on the outcome of the appointment.

Follow Up 1 

Once a proposal has been issued and a first follow up with the customer has been completed move the deal into this stage. If the follow up was a message or email with no response from the customer, leave the deal in Proposal issued until you hear back from the customer.

Schedule a follow up meeting (Shows up on Google calendar) or task with the customer for your next follow date.

Make sure to add notes about the details of the follow up with the customer and list what you discussed.

Follow Up 2 

Once a Follow up has been completed from the Follow Up 1 stage with the customer move the deal into this stage. If the follow up was a message or email with no response from the customer, leave the deal in Proposal issued until you hear back from the customer.

Schedule a follow up meeting (Shows up on Google calendar) or task with the customer for your next follow date.

Make sure to add notes about the details of the follow up with the customer and list what you discussed.

Future Work

Move the deal into this Phase if they plan to move forward with solar beyond 6 months from your follow up.

Make sure to add notes about the details of the follow up with the customer and list what you discussed.

Lender Approval

Customer goes into this stage once they have signed the contract but are waiting on their financing to be approved.

Once their financing is approved, move them into Signed Contract.

Signed Contract

Cash Deal - Once the customer signs a contract, move the deal to Signed Contract.

Financing – Once the customer has signed the contract and financing is approved, move the deal to Signed Contract.

Once a deal has moved into the Signed Contract stage operations are notified and the deal automatically moves into the Operations Pipeline.

DQ/Lost

If the customer is DQ for the following reasons, move them to DQ and enter the reason in the drop-down menu.

  • DQ - Credit Fail
  • DQ - Federal Tax Credit
  • DQ – Shading
  • DQ – Renter
  • Lost – Competitor
  • Lost – Follow Up
  • Lost – Not Interested
  • Lost – One Legger
  • Lost – Price
  • No Show

Activities in HubSpot

Activity

Shows all Deal activity for changes to a stage, notes, emails, calls, tasks, and meetings. 

Notes

Area to keep notes on all communication with the customer and any information about the deal.

Emails

Send all emails to customers through HubSpot. If you have an attachment within the email it will automatically save in Attachments within HubSpot.

Emails sent through HubSpot will show up in the Activity section.

Calls/Texts

Connect with the Aloware software to be able to make calls and send texts from HubSpot and they will automatically record and saved in HubSpot.

  • Download the app. aloware talk app
  • Website to make calls from Computer. https://aloware.com/

Tasks

Area to set up follow ups with customers. They do not show up on your Google calendar.

Create a Task

  • Click Tasks
  • Create Task
  • Name the task in Enter your task.
  • Due Date
  • Send Reminder
  • Type
  • Priority
  • Queue (Follow Up)
  • Assigned to (Sales Rep’s Name)
  • Enter Notes
  • Click the Create button in the lower left corner.
  • Tasks do not show up on your Google calendar.

Meetings

Set up meetings with clients in the Follow Up or Proposal stage.

Create a Meeting

  • Click Meetings
  • Create Meeting
  • Fill in information.
  • Click the Orange Save when finished.
  • Meeting will show up on your Google Calendar.
