Step 1: Locate the Customer

In HubSpot go to the customer’s name under Sales Pipeline then Deal.

Step 2: Verify / Add Information

On the left sidebar scroll down to the Contract Info section and verify or fill in the following information:

  • Customer Name (if blank)
  • Note: Verify the legal name and update if different. Ask to see their Driver's License.
  • Phone Number (if blank)
  • Email (if blank)
  • Street Address (if blank)
  • City (if blank)
  • State (if blank)
  • Zip Code (if blank)
  • Amount Found in Aurora under the Project Name then Designs. $ amount of system.
  • System Size (kw) – Found in Aurora under the Project Name then Designs kilowatts of the system from.
  • # of panels – Found in Aurora under the Project Name then Designs.
  • Panel Manufacture – select from drop down menu.
  • Inverter Type – select from drop down menu.
  • Payment Type -select from drop down menu.
  • Deposit Amount (if applicable)
  • Finance Company – Select from the drop down menu.
  • Estimated 1st Year Production (kWh) – Found in Aurora under Projects, click the bar under Design, in the top right corner click V (drop down menu) and the Energy kWh is the 1st year production.
  • Adders – Examples are battery, trenching, metal roof, etc.
  • Contract Notes (If applicable)
  • Homeowner Name #2 (If applicable)
  • Email #2 (If applicable)
  • Street Address #2 (If applicable)
  • City #2 (If applicable)
  • State #2 (If applicable)
  • Zip Code #2 (If applicable)
  • Click the Orange Save button when finished.

All this information will auto-populate into the Contract, except #2 email. It will need to be entered manually in the contract.

Step 3: Initiate Contract

On the right sidebar under PandoDoc click Create Document.

  • Choose the Cenvar Solar Contract
  • Select the signer's email address from the drop-down menu.
    • Manually enter the 2nd email address, if applicable.
  • Add your email under ADD MORE RECIPIENTS
  • Click Start Editing

Step 4: Review

Review all information for accuracy.

  • If there is only one signer for the contract
    • Double click in the Homeowner, Street Address. City, State, Zip boxes to the right and delete the info.
  • If information is missing, click the vertical ellipses (3 vertical dots) in the upper right corner and click delete.
  • Go back to HubSpot and refresh your page.
  • Fill in missing information in HubSpot from Step 2 and then repeat Step 3.

Step 5: Set up signature(s)

Scroll down to Customer Signatures and fill in Customer Name(s).

  • If only one customer, then double click in the green box and click the trash can icon to delete the green box. You can also enter N/A.
  • Customer will sign within Customer Signature.
  • Date will auto- fill once customer signs.

Step 6: Send for signature(s)

In the top right corner click the Send green button. You have 3 options to choose:

Send via email (will emails the email address in HubSpot).

  • If any required fields have not been completed, then a box pops-up called Unfilled Variables.
    • You may select Review and fill.
    • Replace unfilled variables with blanks.
    • Do not replace (variables will be displayed).
  • Will allow you to name the document.

Share via link.

  • If any required fields have not been completed, then a box pops-up called Unfilled Variables.
    • You may select Review and fill.
    • Replace unfilled variables with blanks.
    • Do not replace (variables will be displayed).
  • Will allow you to name the document.
  • Share link box comes up and click green generate links box to send the contract to the customer with a link through email.

Sign in person (this will allow you to have the customer sign in person).

  • If any required fields have not been completed, then a box pops-up called Unfilled Variables.
    • You may select Review and fill.
    • Replace unfilled variables with blanks.
    • Do not replace (variables will be displayed).
  • Will ask you to confirm the signer.
  • Check the box to confirm.
  • Click Start signing.
  • Click the yellow start box at top.
  • Have the customer(s) enter first & last name.
  • Have the customer sign.
  • Click the yellow finish box at top.
  • Download the document.

Once a contract is signed, move the deal to Signed Contract in HubSpot.
