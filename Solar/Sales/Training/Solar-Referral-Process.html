When the Sales Specialist is provided a referral by someone, they should collect the following information and make sure it gets entered into HubSpot under the Customer’s name in Deals.

  • First & Last Name
  • Address, City, State, Zip Code
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number

In HubSpot

Enter the customer’s deal that was referred.

On the left sidebar scroll down to Referral and enter information:

  • Under Referral select either Yes or No
  • Referrer Name - Enter the first and last name of the person who referred this deal.
  • Referrer Email - Enter the email address of the person who referred this deal.
  • Referrer Address - Enter the address, city, state, zip of the person who referred this deal.
  • Referrer Phone - Enter the phone number xxx-xxx-xxxx of the person who referred this deal.
  • Click the Orange Save button at the bottom of the screen.

In HubSpot - after the appointment

Once the appointment is completed go into HubSpot, on the left sidebar under Appointment Info Select Yes from the drop-down menu in the Appointment Completed field.

At the end of each month Finance will pay out all referrals.

Payout amounts for customer provided referrals are:

  • $100 once appointment completed for non-customers.
  • $200 once appointment is completed for customers.
  • $500 once Solar is paid for customers and non-customers.

Once amount is paid Finance will select the amount paid in the Amount Paid field in the left side bar under Referral

Customers being paid referrals will be paid by check. 

Non-Customers will be paid by check. If the amount is over $600 in a calendar year, they will need to complete a W-9. Collect Information
