Collect Information

When the Sales Specialist is provided a referral by someone, they should collect the following information and make sure it gets entered into HubSpot under the Customer’s name in Deals.

  • First & Last Name
  • Address, City, State, Zip Code
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number

Steps in HubSpot

1. In HubSpot enter the customer’s deal that was referred.

2. On the left sidebar scroll down to Referral and enter information:

  • Under Referral select either Yes or No
  • Referrer Name - Enter the first and last name of the person who referred this deal.
  • Referrer Email - Enter the email address of the person who referred this deal.
  • Referrer Address - Enter the address, city, state, zip of the person who referred this deal.
  • Referrer Phone - Enter the phone number xxx-xxx-xxxx of the person who referred this deal.

3.  Click the Orange Save button at the bottom of the screen.
