Solar - Authority and Responsibility

The basic organizational structure of Cenvar Solar is as follows:

Document can be found: Cenvar Solar Organizational Chart

Finance, Accounting, Human Resources, Information Technology, Safety, Quality Control, and Policies are all functions of the Corporate entity.

Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

  • Company direction
  • New sites
  • Market Trend Monitoring
  • Approve large purchases
  • Finance liaison
  • HR liaison
  • Policy and procedure liaison

Chief Operations Officer (COO)

  • Operations Process Management
  • Quality Control
  • Customer Service
  • Crew Management
  • IT liaison
  • Reports directly to the CEO

Chief Growth Officer (CGO)

  • Business Development
  • Sales
  • Sales Process Management
  • Marketing liaison
  • B2B Sales
  • Reports directly to the CEO

Pipeline Specialist

  • Manages jobs from the point of signed contract through installation
  • On-site for installation
  • Coordinates transfer of information
  • Reports to the COO

Project Coordinator

  • Receive inbound calls
  • Respond to web requests
  • Setup appointments
  • Set up site surveys
  • Reports to the COO
