Initial Call

This is __________ from Cenvar Solar. Is [potential customer name] available? I am calling

regarding your recent inquiry on our website [or other prospecting means]. Is now a good time?

[Pause]

  • “No” = when would be a better time? [schedule a time to call back]
  • “Yes” - Fantastic!

First, I’d like to thank for your inquiry into Cenvar and confirm your contact information [go through name, address, email, phone, etc.]

Before setting up an appointment, is it ok if I ask you a few questions?

● Do you know the approximate age of your roof?

● Do you have a shingle or metal roof?

● What are you interested in solar? (Examples if they are not sure how to respond - Reduce your electric bill, charge your EV car batteries, etc.?) [Record response]

● Which best describes your current level of interest? Would you say you’re gathering information, looking to get better educated, or ready for a purchase? [Record response]

The next step is to set up an appointment. 

(Optional) Do you have a preference for an in-person visit, a video chat or only receive a quote?

We have times available [mention dates/times]. Would any of these work for your schedule?

  • No - suggest other times.
  • Yes - To confirm, our sales representative, [rep name] will be at [their address] on [day of week], [month] [date] at [time].

You will receive a confirmation email; please respond to that email and attach a copy of a recent electric bill. This will make sure we size your solar correctly.

Thank you for considering Cenvar Solar. We look forward to doing business with you.

Information gathered on first call

Age of roof/type of shingle

 Electric bill (or at least know that it’s coming)

 Address and contact information confirmed.
