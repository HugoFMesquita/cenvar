Solar Reviews

  1. Email comes from Solar Review to leads@cenvarsolar.com
    • Solar Review Appt Schedule with Customer’s Name
  2. Verify that the appointment date & time customer set will work for a sales rep.
    • If the appointment works
      1. Call the customer to verify the appointment.
      2. Add a deal to HubSpot and send Sales Rep and Customer Meeting Invite via google calendar.
    • If you have to reschedule the appointment the customer set
      1. Contact the customer to reschedule a date and time.
      2. Enter Deal into HubSpot with the New Appointment Date & Time.
  3. Lead Center Manager will check the Solar Review Dashboard to make sure all appointments have been taken care of; in case an email was missed. 

Roofing Leads

  1. The Lead Center will provide contact information for the lead.
    • Call them to set an appointment.
    • Enter New Deal into HubSpot with Appointment date and time.
  2. Roofing Sales Rep with email customer’s information to leads@cenvarsolar.com.
    • Look up customer in JP to get contact information (if not provided)
    • Call customer to set up an appointment.
    • Add New Deal into HubSpot with Appointment date and time.
