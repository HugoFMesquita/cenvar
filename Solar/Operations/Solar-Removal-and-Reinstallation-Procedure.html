1. Sales Representative utilizes the removal/reinstall calculator to figure the cost of removing solar panels and includes on the removal/reinstall contract.
  2. Customer signs the removal/reinstall contract.
  3. Sales Representative sends signed contract to operations@cenvarsolar.com (this puts Solar on notice immediately).
  4. Roofing Office Manager (OM) notifies solar at least 2 weeks in advance of the anticipated roof replacement schedule. 
  5. Solar schedules and removes the existing system.
  6. Solar notifies the appropriate branch OM when the removal is completed.
  7. Roofing notifies solar when the roof has been replaced and any problems/issues are addressed and corrected.
  8. Solar re-installs the solar system.
