Sales

Prospect reaches out (from marketing, word of mouth, etc.). 

  1. Lead center agent talks to prospect.
  2. Agent books appointment.
  3. Asks if they are interested in solar.

 

If prospect wants quick pitch pricing vs in depth

  1. Let the customer know they can receive a personalized quote to show what it may look like.
  2. Notify customer that internal design specialist who will be designing their system and proposal will be contacting them to prepare it.
  3. The internal design specialist calls the customer and asks them to send a utility bill to meet their needs; without it we cannot put together a proper proposal. If a customer does NOT feel comfortable sending a bill, we attempt to professionally guide the customer over to full solar appointment at this time.  Some customers may feel more comfortable with their personal information only being shared in person.  Roofing estimators are not equipped with time and software access to enter this information properly and guide them through the process of what will fulfill their energy consumption needs.
  4. Internal design specialist also re-emphasizes the bundle discount $0.25/w when customer does roof and solar with Cenvar (must be roof mount).
  5. Internal design specialist builds design in Aurora.
  6. Internal design specialist puts together solar proposal deck.
  7. Internal design specialist sends deck to both customer and roofing estimator.
  8. Internal design specialist notifies customers upon review if they would like to learn more in-depth information, we can do a solar specific appointment and pass on if so.
  9. The roofing estimator takes 10-15 minutes with customers going over the solar proposal.
  10. If customer wants to move forward with solar more in depth, estimator calls lead center agent to get solar appointment booked.
