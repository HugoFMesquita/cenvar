Step by Step Solar Start to Finish

 

Sales

  1. Prospect reaches out (from marketing, word of mouth, etc).
  2. Lead center agent talks to prospect.
  3. Lead center agent books appointment (unless self-generated by rep, then they book their own).
  4. Utility bill is requested at time of booking.
  5. Design is built prior to meeting (if no bill, made with customer).
  6. Sales rep shows customer design and discusses the savings and costs.
  7. Any requested adjustments are made.
  8. Sale is made if customer is prepared (contract signed, finance app if not cash).
  9. Sales rep follows up to answer any additional questions and until customer states they are no longer interested if not sold at time of appointment.

 

Operations

  1. Welcome call made. 
  2. Site survey booked. 
  3. Site survey completed.
  4. Submitted to engineering. 
  5. Approve engineered plans 
  6. Receive stamped engineered plans. 
  7. Permit submitted to AHJ and interconnection submitted to utility. 
  8. Receive approvals from AHJ and utility. 
  9. Schedule install. 
  10. Complete install. 
  11. Schedule AHJ inspection. 
  12. Pass inspection. 
  13. Submit inspection report to utility. 
  14. Utility grants permission to operate.
  15. Activate system. 
  16. Set up customer with monitoring app. 
  17. Monitor system.
