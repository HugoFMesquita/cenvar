Employee Business Referral Policy

Policy Statement 

Our company values the contributions of its employees to expand our customer base and foster growth. As an incentive, we have established a Business Referral Program. This program aims to reward employees for their efforts in identifying and referring potential customers who align with our business goals and values.

Eligibility 

All active, regular full-time and part-time employees are eligible to participate in the Business Referral Program.

Referral Process

  • Employees can refer potential customers who are not currently in our company database.
  • Referrals must be submitted by filling out the Referral Submission Form and submitting the form to management.
  • The above-mentioned form can either be accessed through the company intranet, provided by the HR department, or provided by management.
  • The Referral form must be filled out in its entirety and include relevant information such as the potential customer company name, a point of contact, contact details, and a brief description of their needs or requirements.
  • There is no limit to the number of customers an employee can refer but each referral should be submitted individually.

Eligibility Criteria for Referrals

  • Eligible referred customers are ones who are not currently in our company database and/or,
  • Eligible referred customers must not be existing leads, prospects, or current customers of our company.
  • The referred customer must order 2 or more orders or $5,000 in pre-tax dollar amount for the employee to be eligible for the bonus/fee.
  • No sale or order is bonus eligible until the company has received payment in full.
  • The referral should lead to a qualified sales opportunity, which is determined by the sales and business development teams.

Reward Structure

  • The bonus will be calculated as 1.5% of the customers total gross pre-tax sales of a quarter.
  • Sales eligible for bonusing for the quarter must have had payments received against them.
    • Any orders not paid for will be eligible for bonus upon future payment and will be paid out on the following scheduled bonus payment.

Payment and Taxation

  • All bonuses are subject to applicable tax deductions and regulations.
  • Payment of the bonus will be processed through the company's standard payroll system.
  • The bonus will be paid out on the third paycheck of the month following each quarter.

Conflict of Interest

Employees are prohibited from referring potential customers who are already affiliated with employee's personal business interests or who could create a conflict of interest with the company. Management has final determination on any potential conflicts of interest and reserve the right to exclude any potential customer or sales from the bonus structure.  Failure to comply with this policy could result in disciplinary action.

Policy Enforcement

Violation of this policy, including fraudulent or deceptive referrals, will result in the employee's disqualification from the Business Referral Program and may lead to further disciplinary actions as per the company's policies.

Policy Review

This policy will be reviewed periodically and may be updated as necessary to align with the company's evolving business needs and objectives. Additionally, this policy does not exist in perpetuity and can be terminated at any time. If management determines to terminate the policy, any customer referrals established while the policy is active will be honored in perpetuity. 

By participating in the Business Referral Program, employees acknowledge and agree to abide by the terms and conditions outlined in this policy.

Approval
